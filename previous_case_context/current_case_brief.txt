# CURRENT DISPUTE: Lismore Capital vs Process Holdings Ltd

## Core Issue
Process Holdings claims they didn't know about P&ID wrongdoing.
Lismore claims Process Holdings DID know and are liable.

## Lismore's Position
- Process Holdings failed to honor guarantees about collaborative enforcement
- Process Holdings' response to fraud finding damaged investment value
- Lismore acquired stake POST-award, not involved in original fraud
- Process Holdings should have known risks ("vulture fund" should do DD)

## Process Holdings' Position  
- Lismore misrepresented award legitimacy
- Lismore blocked audits and discovery
- Lismore failed shareholder guarantees
- Investment was solicited under misleading conditions

## Key Legal Questions
1. Who bears responsibility for failed enforcement after fraud finding?
2. Did Process Holdings have constructive knowledge of fraud?
3. Did Lismore misrepresent or did Process Holdings fail in due diligence?
4. What damages flow from the collapsed P&ID award?
