Chapter 25 - Supply of Goods and Digital Content
Buyer’s Defences
Defences to claims in respect of goods sold on approval or on sale or return
To be entered as s.3 of Form N9 or attached as a separate document
1.
25-F58
The Defendant admits that the Claimant supplied it with the goods referred in the Particulars of Claim on approval.
2.
The Defendant denies that he has approved the goods either as alleged in the Particulars of Claim or at all. On [date]
the Defendant orally notified the Claimant that he was rejecting the goods and on [date] returned them to the Claimant’s
premises.
Or
1.
The Defendant admits that the Claimant supplied it with the goods referred in the Particulars of Claim on sale or return,
namely on terms that the Defendant should be at liberty to return to the Claimant within 90 days such goods as he did not
adopt and should pay for only such goods, if any as were not returned by him within such time.
2.
The Defendant denies that he adopted the goods either as alleged in the Particulars of Claim or at all. On [date] prior to
the expiry of the 90 days, the Defendant attempted to redeliver the goods to the Claimant but the Claimant wrongfully
refused to accept them.
3.
In the premises, there was never a contract of sale between the Claimant and the Defendant and the Claimant is not entitled
to payment of the alleged or any sum.
