Claim based on trespass to goods (damage/destruction)
Particulars of claim to be attached in Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
26-F13
The Claimant is the owner of [identify goods] (“the goods”).
2.
On [date], the Defendant, with the intention of damaging the goods, picked them up and smashed them.
3.
In the premises, the Claimant was guilty of trespass.
4.
By reason of the trespass, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage.
Particulars
 
(1)The cost of repair was £[..........].
 
(2)The goods in their undamaged condition were worth £[..........]. In their present condition, although repaired, they
are worth no more than £[..........]. The diminution in value amounts to £[..........].
5.
The Claimant is entitled to interest at such rate as the Court deems just pursuant to [s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984]
[s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981].
The Claimant therefore claims:
 
(1)Damages.
 
(2)Interest under [s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984] [s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981].
