Defence
1.
18-E6
The Defendant admits that:
 
(1)he executed the Guarantee dated [date]; and
 
(2)demand was made for payment by the Claimant, B Bank Plc, on [date] and no payment has been made by the
Defendant.
2.
The Defendant avers that the execution of the Guarantee was procured by the undue influence of [name].
3.
[Insert particulars of undue influence, including the nature of any presumption, legal or evidential, relied upon and the
basis for it].
4.
The Claimant had [actual/constructive] notice of the fact that the execution of the Guarantee was procured by the exercise
of undue influence.
5.
[Insert details of actual/constructive notice. Note that in all non-commercial surety cases, if the Bank/lender has failed to
take the Etridge reasonable steps, it will have constructive notice of any undue influence which is made out.]

Counterclaim
7.
The Defence above is repeated by way of counterclaim.
AND the Defendant counterclaims:
 
(1)A declaration that the Guarantee has been rescinded/set aside as having been procured by the undue influence of
[..........] and that the Claimant had [actual/constructive] notice of the undue influence.
 
(2)An order that the Claimant do deliver up the Guarantee to be cancelled forthwith.
