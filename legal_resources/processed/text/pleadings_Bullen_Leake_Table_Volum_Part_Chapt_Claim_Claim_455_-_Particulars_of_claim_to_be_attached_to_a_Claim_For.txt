27-F4
The Claimant and the Defendant are directors of XY Limited.
2.
By an agreement in writing (“the supply agreement”) dated [date] made between PQ Limited and XY Limited, PQ Limited
agreed to supply [identify the goods to be supplied] to XY Limited. A copy of the supply agreement is annexed to this
Claim Form.
3.
By a guarantee in writing (“the guarantee”) dated [date] made between the Claimant and the Defendant as guarantors and
PQ Limited as supplier, the Claimant and the Defendant agreed jointly and severally to guarantee all sums which might
become due from XY Limited to PQ Limited under the supply agreement. A copy of the guarantee is annexed to this
Claim Form.
4.
XY Limited failed to pay sums due to PQ Limited under the supply agreement and on [date], there was due and owing
to PQ Limited the sum of £[..........].
5.
On [date] a resolution was passed by the creditors of XY Limited for the winding-up of the company.
6.
By a letter dated [date] to the Claimant and the Defendant PQ Limited duly demanded payment of the said sum of £[..........]
pursuant to the guarantee.
7.
On [date], the Claimant paid the full sum of £[..........] to PQ Limited in discharge of the demand.
