Section 7 - Legal Representatives—Costs and Litigation Funding
Section 7C - The Solicitors Act 1974
Solicitors’ (Non Contentious Business) Remuneration Order 1994
5.— Disciplinary and other measures
7C-198+
(1) If on a taxation the taxing officer allows less than one half of the costs, he must bring the facts of the case to
the attention of the Council.
(2) The provisions of this Order are without prejudice to the general powers of the Council under the Solicitors Act
1974.
