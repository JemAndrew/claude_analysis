Article 17 refers only to the Tribunal. There can be, however, no doubt that an Emergency Arbitrator has the power to ask
counsel of the applicant, for instance, for emergency measures to furnish proof of authority. Article 17 would be applicable by
analogy, certainly via art.8(3) of the Emergency Arbitrator Rules, which allows the Emergency Arbitrator to act in all matters
concerning emergency arbitrator proceedings not expressly provided for in the Appendix, “in the spirit of the Rules”. 22
