FSMA s.22 defines regulated activities by reference to investments of a “specified kind” and “specified benchmarks”. FSMA
s.22(5) gives the Treasury the power to specify investments and benchmarks. Sch.2 Pt II to FSMA contains an indicative list
of investments which may be specified. The investments are specified by the Treasury in Pt III of the RAO. The investments
specified in the RAO at the time of writing are listed in summary form below. Reference should be made to the RAO 80  for the
precise and detailed description of the investments and exclusions.
 
(i)deposits 81 ;
 
(ii)electronic money 82 ;
 
(iii)rights under a contract of insurance 83 ;
 
(iv)shares or stock in the capital of any body corporate (wherever incorporated) or in any unincorporated body constituted
under the laws of a country or territory outside the UK 84 ;
 
(v)instruments creating or acknowledging indebtedness, including debentures, debenture stock, loan stock, bonds, and
certificates of deposit 85 ;
 
(vi)rights under an alternative finance investment bond 86 ;
 
(vii)loan stock, bonds and other instruments creating or acknowledging indebtedness issued by governments, UK local
authorities etc. 87 ;
 
(viii)warrants and other instruments entitling the holder to subscribe for any investment of the kind in (iv)–(vii) 88 ;
 
(ix)certificates giving contractual or property rights to an investment held by another 89 ;
 
(x)units in a collective investment scheme 90 ;
 
(xi)rights under a pension scheme 91 ;
 
(xii)greenhouse gas emissions allowances 92 ;
 
(xiii)emission allowances 93 ;
 
(xiv)options to acquire or dispose of specified assets (including a security or contractually based investment, currency of
the UK or any other territory, palladium, platinum, gold or silver) 94 ;
 
(xv)futures (i.e. rights under a contract for the sale of a commodity or property of any other description under which
delivery is to be made at a future date and at a price agreed on when the contract is made) 95 ;

(xvi)rights under contracts for differences and any other contract intended or purportedly intended to avoid a loss or secure
a profit by reference to fluctuations in the value of property or an index 96 ;
 
(xvii)the underwriting capacity of a Lloyd’s syndicate and membership or prospective membership of such a syndicate 97 ;
 
(xviii)rights under a funeral plan 98 ;
 
(xix)rights under a regulated mortgage contract 99 ;
 
(xx)rights under a regulated home reversion plan 100 ;
 
(xxi)rights under a regulated home purchase plan 101 ;
 
(xxii)rights under a regulated sale and rent back agreement 102 ;
 
(xxiii)rights under a credit agreement 103 ;
 
(xxiv)rights under a consumer hire agreement 104 ; and
 
(xxv)any right to or interest in other specified investments. 105
The specified benchmarks are set out in Sch.5 to the RAO. At the time of writing, these are the LIBOR, ISDAFIX, Sterling
Overnight Index Average (SONIA), Repurchase Overnight Index Average (RONIA), WM/Reuters London 4 pm Closing Spot
Rate, London Gold Fixing, LBMA Silver Price and ICE Brent Index.
