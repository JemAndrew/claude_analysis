Regulation (EU) No.606/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 12 June 2013 on mutual recognition of
protection measures in civil matters
Chapter II Recognition and Enforcement of Protection Measures
Article 14 Suspension or withdrawal of recognition or enforcement
5-167+
1. [Omitted]
2. Upon submission by the protected person or the person causing the risk of the certificate issued in accordance with
Article 14(1) of the MRP Regulation, the competent court shall suspend or withdraw the effects of the recognition
and, where applicable, the enforcement of the protection measure.
