Article 17(3): Proof of authority requested by the Secretariat
“At any time after the commencement of the arbitration, the arbitral tribunal or the Secretariat may require proof of the
authority of any party representatives.”
17-9
The Secretariat may request proof of authority to represent a party if it receives a Request (or any other pleading) and it is not
clear for some reason whether the person transmitting it has authority to represent the party involved. The Secretariat may raise
the issue spontaneously, but is more likely to do so only if the issue is raised by another party. 19  However, under art.17(3), the
Secretariat can require proof of authority which would cover proof of corporate authorization.
