Chapter 5 - The Arbitral Proceedings
The Term “Emergency Arbitrator”
29-15
The Emergency Arbitrator Provisions use the term “arbitrator” to designate the Emergency Arbitrator. The Emergency Arbitrator
is called upon to adjudicate a request for interim measures. The Emergency Arbitrator has all the attributes of an arbitrator and
for all purposes should be treated as such. 546  The Emergency Arbitrator is not a mediator or settlement facilitator, and although
their mandate is similar, albeit narrower than that of a dispute board adjudicator or of a pre-arbitral referee, they are neither an
adjudicator nor a referee, but an arbitrator with a very specific and limited mandate. 547  The Emergency Arbitrator can only
provide temporary relief, and cannot, of course, decide the merits of the dispute (which he or she should not prejudge either).
