Right of Tribunal to Revisit Emergency Arbitrator’s Order
Article 29(5)
“Articles 29(1)–29(4) and the Emergency Arbitrator Rules set forth in Appendix V (collectively the “Emergency Arbitrator
Provisions”) shall apply only to parties that are either signatories of the arbitration agreement under the Rules that is relied
upon for the application or successors to such signatories.”
