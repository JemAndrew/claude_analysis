Seller’s Defences
Denial of express condition or warranty and of misrepresentation
To be entered as s.3 of Form N9 or attached as a separate document
1.
25-F38
The Defendant admits that he entered into the oral agreement with the Claimant whereby the Defendant agreed to sell and
deliver the goods referred to in the Particulars of Claim.
2.
The Defendant denies that the agreement contained the express condition alleged in paragraph X of the Particulars of
Claim or any condition to the like effect. [If the Defendant makes a positive case that a different condition was agreed,
this should be stated here.]
3.
The Defendant denies that he gave the warranty alleged in the Particulars of Claim or any warranty to the like effect. The
Defendant further denies that there was or would have been any consideration for any such warranty.
4.
The Defendant denies each of the representations alleged to have been made by him and denies that he made any
representations to the like effect.
