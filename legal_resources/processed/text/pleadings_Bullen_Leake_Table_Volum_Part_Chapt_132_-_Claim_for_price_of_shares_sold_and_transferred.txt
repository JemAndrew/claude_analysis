Claim for price of shares sold and transferred
1.
16-E1
On [date] the defendant agreed with the claimant by an agreement made [in writing dated [date] or as the case may be] to
purchase from the claimant 500 ordinary shares of £1 each in A.B. Limited at the price of £1,500.
2.
On the [date] pursuant to the said agreement the claimant sold and transferred the said shares to the defendant but the
defendant has not paid the said price or any part thereof.
3.
The claimant will claim interest on the said sum pursuant to s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981 at the rate of 15 per cent
per annum from [date for completion of the sale] amounting to [amount] at the date hereof and continuing until judgment
or sooner payment at the rate of [amount] per day.
AND the claimant claims the sum of £1,500 and [amount] for interest and continuing interest under paragraph 3 above.
