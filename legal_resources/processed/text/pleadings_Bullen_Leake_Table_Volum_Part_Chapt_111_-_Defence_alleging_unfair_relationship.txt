Defence alleging unfair relationship
1.
14-E7
The Defendant is an individual.
2.
It is admitted that the Claimant agreed to lend and the Defendant agreed to repay the sum of £[..........] as alleged in the
Particulars of Claim (“the Loan Agreement”).
3.
The Loan Agreement is a credit agreement within the meaning of section 140C(1) of the Consumer Credit Act 1974 (“the
Act”) to which the provisions of sections 140A-140D of the Act apply.
4.
The relationship between the Claimant and the Defendant is unfair within the meaning of section 140A of the Act, because
[set out particulars].
5.
Accordingly, by section 140B(9) of the Act, it is for the Claimant to prove that the relationship is fair.
6.
In the premises, the Defendant is entitled under section 140B of the Act to be relieved from the indebtedness under the
Loan Agreement [in whole or in part].
