“as soon as the file has been transmitted to [the Tribunal]”
28-15
The file is transmitted to the Tribunal pursuant to art.16 once it has been constituted and the initial provisional advance on
costs has been paid. The Tribunal would thus be able to order provisional measures as soon as it has received the file from the
Secretariat, subject, however, to possible mandatory provisions of the law of the place of arbitration or exclusive jurisdiction
of the courts. 470  In particular, there is no reason why the Tribunal would first have to establish Terms of Reference, even when
it wishes to render its decision in the form of an award rather than procedural order. 471  Before the file has been transmitted to
the Tribunal a party may apply to an Emergency Arbitration if the conditions of art.29 are met.
