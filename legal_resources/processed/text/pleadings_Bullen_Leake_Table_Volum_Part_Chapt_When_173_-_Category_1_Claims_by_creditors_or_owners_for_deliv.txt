Category 1: Claims by creditors or owners for delivery of goods under unregulated hire-purchase and conditional sale
agreements (non-corporate defendant)
20-43
These proceedings may be started either in the High Court or the county court on ordinary Part 7 principles. CCA Procedure
does not apply.
The form and content of the Claim Form are, however, strictly regulated. The applicable procedure is to be found in CPR PD
16.6. Where the action is for the delivery of goods to enforce a hire-purchase or conditional sale agreement, the claim must
contain the prescribed information (in the right order) as set out in CPR PD 16.6.1.
Note that these rules do not apply to credit-sale agreements nor do they apply to any kind of hire agreement.
