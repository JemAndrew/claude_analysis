Claim against principal debtor and guarantor on a guarantee for the price of goods sold/supplied
1.
13-E1
The Claimant is a motor vehicle dealer. The First Defendant/Principal Debtor agreed to purchase a vehicle, registration
number [..........] (“the vehicle”) from the Claimant, by an agreement made in writing dated [date] (“the sale agreement”)
for the price of £[..........]. A copy of the sale agreement is attached as “A” to these Particulars of Claim. 40
2.
By a guarantee dated [date] made in writing between the Claimant and both Defendants (“the Guarantee”), and in
consideration of the Claimant entering into the sale agreement, the Second Defendant/the Guarantor, guaranteed the
payment of £[..........] by the First Defendant/Principal Debtor to the Claimant. A copy of the sale agreement is attached
as “B” to these Particulars of Claim. 41
3.
The Claimant delivered the vehicle to the First Defendant at his home address on [date], the date for delivery specified
in the sale agreement.
4.
In accordance with the payment terms set out at clause [..........] of the sale agreement, the Claimant requested payment of
the sum of £[..........] from the First Defendant on [date].
5.
In breach of the sale agreement, the First Defendant has failed to make the requested (or any) payment to the Claimant
despite having taken delivery of the vehicle.
6.
Further, by a letter dated [date] the Claimant made demand on the Second Defendant under the terms of the Guarantee
for the sum of £[..........].
7.
The Second Defendant has failed to make payment of the sum demanded, or any sum.
