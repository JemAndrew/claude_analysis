Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
20-F15
By an agreement in writing dated [date], the Claimant advanced to the Defendant the sum of £[..........]. A copy of the
agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
The said loan with interest of £[..........] was payable by x equal consecutive monthly instalments of £[..........].
3.
By clause 6, the agreement provided that, if the Defendant failed to pay any instalment on the due date, the Claimant
should be entitled to serve a notice terminating the loan whereupon the balance of the loan and interest should immediately
become payable by the Defendant.
4.
The Defendant failed to pay the instalments due from [date] to [date] in the total sum of £[..........]. On [date] the instalments
were £[..........] in arrear.
5.
By a notice dated [date] (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim), the Claimant as it was entitled to do duly terminated
the loan. In consequence the balance of the loan and interest became payable by the Defendant in the sum of £[..........].
6.
The Defendant has failed to pay the arrears of £[..........] or the balance due of £[..........] or any part thereof.
The Claimant therefore claims:
 
(1)£[..........] arrears of instalments.
 
(2)£[..........] balance of the loan and charges.
