Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
25-F8
The Claimant carries on business as printer and the Defendant carries on business as a publisher of magazines.
2.
By a contract in writing made [date], the Claimant agreed to print for the Defendant x copies of a magazine to be entitled
[give title] at a cost of £[..........].
3.
The contract was concluded in a faxed letter from the Defendant to the Claimant dated [date] and an acceptance of order
form sent by the Claimant to the Defendant dated [date]. Copies of those documents are annexed to these Particulars of
Claim.
4.
In pursuance of the contract, on [date] the Claimant began work on printing the magazines.
5.
On [date] when the work was still in progress, the Defendant in breach of contract informed the Claimant that it no longer
wanted the magazines as its market research had revealed a lack of interest in the proposed magazine. The Defendant stated
that it would neither accept delivery of nor pay for the magazines ordered. This information was conveyed in a message
sent by email (a copy of the printout of the email is annexed to these Particulars of Claim).
6.
On receipt of the email, the Claimant telephoned the Defendant who reiterated the Defendant’s refusal to continue with
the contract. In the circumstances, the Defendant repudiated the contract and the Claimant accepted that repudiation in
the telephone conversation.
7.
By reason of the Defendant’s breach and repudiation of the contract, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage.
