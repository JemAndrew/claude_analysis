Defence by joint maker alleging that he was accommodation party only, and that the Claimant, knowing this, gave
time to the other maker
1.
10-E27
It is admitted that the Defendant together with AB made and delivered to CD the Note sued upon.
2.
It is admitted that CD indorsed the Note to the Claimant who became a holder of it.
3.
Thereafter, the Defendant advised the Claimant, as was the case, that he received no consideration for the Note, but had
signed it at AB’s request and for AB’s accommodation only.
4.
Despite that, and without the consent of the Defendant, the Claimant thereafter and in consideration of [..........] agreed to
give time to AB. In the premises the Defendant was discharged from liability upon the Note.
