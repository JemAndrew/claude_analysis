When the “Consumer Credit Act Procedure” (“CCA Procedure”) applies
Category 6: Claims by creditors or owners for enforcement orders
20-48
The relevant procedure is to be found in CPR PD 7B.7.4.
The claim can simply ask for an order permitting enforcement or, which would much more usual, can claim for substantive
relief (possession of goods or a money judgment) coupled with a claim for an enforcement order. In either case, the claimant
must state in his particulars of claim the circumstances requiring him to obtain an enforcement order.
The CCA Procedure applies so that the particulars of claim must be served with the claim form which must include or be
accompanied by a notice of the hearing date.
