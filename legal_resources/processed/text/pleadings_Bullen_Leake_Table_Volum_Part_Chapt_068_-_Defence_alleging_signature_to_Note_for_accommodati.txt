Defence alleging signature to Note for accommodation of the Claimant and denying consideration
1.
10-E26
The Defendant made and delivered to the Claimant the Note at the Claimant’s request and for his accommodation, in order
that he could discount it.
2.
There was no consideration for the Defendant’s signature to the Note and it is therefore denied that the Defendant is liable
to the Claimant upon it as alleged or at all.
