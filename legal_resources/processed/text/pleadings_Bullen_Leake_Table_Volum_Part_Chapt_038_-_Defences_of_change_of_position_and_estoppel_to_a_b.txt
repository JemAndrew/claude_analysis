Defences of change of position and estoppel to a bank’sclaim for restitution of a mistaken payment
1.
9-E11
It is admitted that on [date] the Claimant paid to the Defendant and the Defendant received the sum of [..........] by way
of a CHAPS transfer (“the Payment”).
2.
The Defendant has by reason of the Payment changed its position and it would be inequitable to require the Defendant to
repay the amount of payment or any part of it. [Set out particulars].
3.
Further or alternatively, prior to the receipt of the banker’s draft, the Claimant represented to the Defendant that the Payment
was due to the Defendant and/or that the Defendant had good title to the Payment. [Set out particulars.]
4.
In reliance upon that representation, the Defendant changed his position as set out above.
5.
Further or alternatively, the Claimant is estopped from reclaiming the Payment.
6.
For these reasons, it is denied that the Claimant is entitled to restitution of the sum claimed or any sum.
