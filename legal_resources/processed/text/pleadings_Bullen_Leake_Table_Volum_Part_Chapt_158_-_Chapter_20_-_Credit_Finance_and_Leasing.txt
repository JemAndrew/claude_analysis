Chapter 20 - Credit, Finance and Leasing
20-01
This section is designed to deal with most aspects of credit and with the acquisition of goods or the use of goods by means
other than outright purchase. It will therefore cover:
 
1.Contracts where goods are acquired on credit:
 
(a)hire-purchase;
 
(b)conditional sale;
 
(c)credit sale.
 
2.Hire and leasing of goods.
 
3.Loans and other forms of financial accommodation.
 
4.Credit cards and charge cards.
The length of the text preceding the forms is due to the horrendous complexity of both the underlying law and the procedural
requirements of agreements in this field. Readers are requested to study the material on procedure below before using the forms
and to check, where recommended in that material, that the procedure remains unchanged from that in the text.
Omissions
20-02
The section will not deal with two areas of credit and financing which are covered elsewhere in this work:
 
1.Mortgages.
 
2.Corporate financing, including debentures and other forms of charge on corporate assets.
