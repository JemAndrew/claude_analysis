Immediately upon appointment of the Emergency Arbitrator by the ICC Court’s President, the Secretariat will send the file to
the Emergency Arbitrator, art.2(3) of the Emergency Arbitrator Rules. Up to this stage the “responding party” will not have
been requested by the ICC to state its position in respect of the Application, as will be discussed further below.
