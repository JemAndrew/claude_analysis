The Claimant is a wholesale dealer in [identify product] and the Defendant is an importer of grain.
2.
By a contract in writing dated [date], the Defendant agreed to sell to the Claimant x tons of [identify the product] (“the
goods”) at a price of £[..........] per ton, to be delivered to the Claimant’s premises at [address] on [date].
3.
The contract was concluded in an exchange of telex messages passing between the Claimant and the Defendant on [date].
Copies of those documents are annexed to these Particulars of Claim.
4.
At the time of the contract, the Defendant well knew that the Claimant was purchasing the goods for onward sale in the
course of his business.
5.
In breach of contract, the Defendant failed to deliver the goods or any part thereof on [date]. The Defendant delivered x
tons on [date 1] and the balance on [date 2].
6.
By reason of the Defendant’s breach of contract, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage.
