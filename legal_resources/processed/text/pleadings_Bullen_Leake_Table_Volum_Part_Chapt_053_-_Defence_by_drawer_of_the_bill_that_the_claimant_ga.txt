Defence by drawer of the bill that the claimant gave time to the acceptor
1.
10-E11
It is admitted that the Defendant drew the bill sued upon, and that the bill was accepted by XY Ltd but then dishonoured
as alleged.
2.
Following that dishonour, the Claimant agreed with XY Ltd [in writing, or orally setting out necessary details of the
agreement] that XY Ltd should have a further six months in which to pay the amount of the bill.
3.
That agreement was made without the knowledge or assent of the Defendant.
4.
In the circumstances the Defendant is discharged from liability upon the bill.
