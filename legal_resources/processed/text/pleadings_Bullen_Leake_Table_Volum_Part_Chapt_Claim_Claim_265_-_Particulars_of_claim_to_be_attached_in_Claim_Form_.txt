1.
21-F4
The Claimant is an estate agent.
2.
By an agreement contained in or evidenced by a letter dated [date] from the Claimant to the Defendant, the Claimant agreed
to act for the Defendant as his sole selling agent in the marketing of his property known as [identify the property]. A copy
of the letter is annexed to this Claim form.
3.
The following were express terms of the contract:
 
(i)that in the event of the Claimant introducing a person who was ready willing and able to purchase the property, the
Claimant was entitled to be paid commission at the rate of x% of the agreed price;
 
(ii)that the Claimant was permitted the opportunity of being the sole selling agent for a y month period ending on
[date].
4.
In [date], the Defendant sold the property through the agency of the [identify the other agency involved] at a price of
£[..........] [or at a price unknown to the Claimant prior to disclosure].
5.
In selling the property other than through the agency of the Claimant, the Defendant has suffered loss and damage, that is,
commission at x% of £[..........], namely £[..........], [or x% of the price] alternatively, damages for breach of contract.
6.
The Claimant is entitled to interest pursuant to [s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984] [s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981]
at such rate and for such period as the Court sees fit.
