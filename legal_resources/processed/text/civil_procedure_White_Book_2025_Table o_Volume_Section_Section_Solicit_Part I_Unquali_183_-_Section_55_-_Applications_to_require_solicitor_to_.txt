Section 7C - The Solicitors Act 1974
Solicitors Act 1974
Section 55
Part I
Unqualified persons acting as solicitors
55. Applications to require solicitor to answer allegations
7C-17
For the avoidance of doubt it is hereby declared that an application by any person to require a solicitor to answer allegations
contained in an affidavit, whether that application is made to the Tribunal or to the High Court, may be treated as an
application to strike the name of that solicitor off the roll on the grounds of the matters alleged.
