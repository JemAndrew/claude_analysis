Regulations under the Legal Aid Sentencing and Punishment of Offenders Act 2012
Civil Legal Aid (Statutory Charge) Regulations 2013
Part 3 Enforcement of the Statutory Charge
24.— Review of postponement
7D-101+
(1) The Lord Chancellor may review a decision to postpone the enforcement of the statutory charge at any time and
where on doing so considers that the conditions in regulation 22(1) are no longer satisfied, the Lord Chancellor must
enforce the charge.
(2) Where after a review the Lord Chancellor further postpones enforcement of the statutory charge—
(a) it may be postponed on such terms and conditions as to repayment by way of interim payments of either
capital or interest, or both, as appear to the Lord Chancellor appropriate; and
(b) interest shall continue to accrue in accordance with regulation 25 .
