Claim on a common money bond
1.
11-E1
On [date], the Defendant executed a bond by which he bound himself to pay to the Claimant the sum of £10,000 (“the
Bond”). The Bond was subject to the condition that if the Defendant paid the Claimant the sum of £5,000 on [date] plus
interest at the rate of [..........] per cent from [date], the Bond would be void. A copy of the Bond is attached to these
Particulars of Claim marked “A”.
2.
The Defendant did not pay the sum of £5,000 or any part of it by [date] or at all.
3.
Accordingly, the Claimant is entitled to and claims the principal sum of £5,000 plus interest at the agreed rate from [date]
to [date], amounting to £[..........] at [date] and continuing at the daily rate of £[..........] until judgment or sooner payment.
4.
Further or alternatively, the Claimant is entitled to and claims interest at such rate and for such period as the court may
determine pursuant to s.35A Senior Courts Act 1981.
AND the Claimant claims:
 
(1)£5,000;
 
(2)Interest at the contractual rate, under paragraph 3 above;
 
(3)Further or alternatively, interest pursuant to s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981 to be assessed.
