Section 7 - Legal Representatives—Costs and Litigation Funding
Section 7C - The Solicitors Act 1974
Solicitors’ (Non-Contentious Business) Remuneration Order 2009
4. Security for costs
7C-214.
1
A solicitor may take from his client security for the payment of any costs, including the amount of any interest to which
the solicitor may become entitled under article 5.
