Part I - Arbitration Rules
Chapter 5 - The Arbitral Proceedings
Right of Emergency Arbitrator to Act as Arbitrator
Article 29(3)
“The emergency arbitrator’s order shall not bind the arbitral tribunal with respect to any question, issue or dispute
determined in the order. The arbitral tribunal may modify, terminate or annul the order or any modification thereto made
by the emergency arbitrator.”
