Section 5 - International Jurisdiction and Enforcement of Judgments
Part - Legislation
Civil Jurisdiction and Judgments Act 1982 (Gibraltar) Order 1997
Article 5
5.
5-105
A judgment shall not be recognised under this Order if, had it been given in another Contracting State, recognition would
be refused by virtue of an agreement to which Article 59 of the 1968 Convention applies.
