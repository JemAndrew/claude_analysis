Defence alleging that sum lent was less than claimed (and tender before action)
1.
14-E4
It is denied that the Claimant lent to the Defendant the sum of £60,000 claimed.
2.
Rather, on or around [date], the Claimant lent to the Defendant the sum of £30,000 and no more.
3.
On [date], before the issue of the Claim Form, the Defendant tendered but the Claimant refused to accept the sum of
£30,000.
4.
The Defendant has now paid the sum of £30,000 into court.
