The table below reproduces an approximate timeline of the expedited procedures: 646
30-12
For the Secretariat of the ICC Court, administrating expedited procedures is particularly challenging at the inception of the
arbitration and once the draft award is received for scrutiny. In the past, too often the Secretariat, and by extension, the ICC
Court have been a cause for delay when it comes to the approval of awards. Given the intent behind art.30, delays at the ICC’s
level will have to be avoided as much as possible in “small” case proceedings. In the 2021 ICC Note, the ICC promises that
awards rendered pursuant to the Expedited Procedure Provisions will be scrutinized “as soon as possible, and in any event no
later than two to three weeks of receipt by the Secretariat”, as against three to four weeks for any other awards that need not
to go before a Special Committee of the Court.
30-13
The timeline in this table also makes plain that claimants pursuing “small” claims are well advised to prepare the Request for
Arbitration as if it were the Statement of Claim, while respondents will need to treat the Answer as the Statements of Defense
(with the possibility to request and obtain an extension of the time-limit to file the Answer in accordance with art.5(2) of the
Rules). The limited time to complete the arbitration proceedings under the Expedited Procedure Provisions reduces both the
time available to file a brief but also the number of submissions that can be exchanged. It is therefore essential for parties to
better prepare their cases upfront.
30-14
A new art.4(6) has been added to App.II stating that “for the purpose of expedited procedures … the Court may exceptionally
establish a Committee consisting of one member.” 647  One may question the effectiveness of this change, as it may impact the
quality of the review of the award, without delivering a significant saving of time to the approval process. 648  Pursuant to the
2021 ICC Note, the scrutiny by a one-member Committee is to take place in exceptional circumstances only.
