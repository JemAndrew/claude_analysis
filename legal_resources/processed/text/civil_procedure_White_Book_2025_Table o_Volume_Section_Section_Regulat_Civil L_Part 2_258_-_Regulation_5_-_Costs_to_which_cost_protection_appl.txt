Regulations under the Legal Aid Sentencing and Punishment of Offenders Act 2012
Civil Legal Aid (Costs) Regulations 2013
Part 2 Cost Protection
5. Costs to which cost protection applies
7D-108+
Except as provided by this Part, cost protection only applies to costs incurred by the receiving party in relation to
proceedings which are, as regards the legally aided party, relevant proceedings.
