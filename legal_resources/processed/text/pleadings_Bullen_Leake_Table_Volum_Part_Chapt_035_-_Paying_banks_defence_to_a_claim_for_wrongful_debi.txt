Paying bank’s defence to a claim for wrongful debit (payment on a forged signature), alleging customer’s failure to
inform the bank of the forgery
1.
9-E8
It is admitted that the Defendant paid the Cheque and debited the Account with the amount of the Cheque.
2.
Prior to [the date on which the Cheque was paid], the Claimant had discovered the forgery and had failed to inform the
Defendant.
3.
The Claimant is estopped by his silence from asserting the forgery. Accordingly, the Defendant was entitled so to debit
the Account.
