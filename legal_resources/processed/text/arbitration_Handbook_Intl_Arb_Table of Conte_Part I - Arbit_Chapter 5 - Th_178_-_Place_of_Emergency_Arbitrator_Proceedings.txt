Part I - Arbitration Rules
Chapter 5 - The Arbitral Proceedings
Place of Emergency Arbitrator Proceedings
29-49
The place of Emergency Arbitrator Proceedings shall be identical to the place of arbitration agreed upon by the parties in the
arbitration agreement or otherwise, see art.4(1) of the Emergency Arbitrator Rules. If no agreement exists, it is incumbent upon
the President of the ICC Court to fix such a place. Although the Emergency Arbitrator Provisions do not say precisely, the
President will, if necessary, fix the place at the time of appointing the Emergency Arbitrator.
29-50
Pursuant to the annual case statistics of the ICC Court, for many years now, in well over 90 per cent of the cases parties to ICC
arbitrations have agreed upon the place of arbitration. 573  Accordingly, the need for the President of the ICC Court to fix the
place of Emergency Arbitrator Proceedings should not arise often. In the first 80 Emergency Arbitrator Proceedings, it arose
on five occasions but in two of those there was an issue as to the city where the reference was to “New York” or the “State
of New York”. 574
29-51
Under art.18(1) of the Rules, the ICC Court fixes the place of arbitration (unless agreed upon by the parties). The criteria taken
into consideration by the ICC Court in doing so will apply mutatis mutandis to the ICC Court’s President when fixing the place
of Emergency Arbitrator Proceedings.
29-52
There is, however, a significant difference between the place of arbitration and the place of Emergency Arbitrator Proceedings.
Unlike the latter, the former has a legal significance when it comes to seeking the annulment and/or the enforcement of an
award. 575  National arbitration laws and always the New York Convention expressly refer to the place of arbitration and attach
legal consequences to its existence in any given dispute. 576
29-53
Nevertheless, the concept of place of arbitration could be relevant if a party seeks an injunction or other relief from a state court
with respect to the Emergency Arbitrator Proceedings. For example, if there is a challenge to the Emergency Arbitrator, the
issue may arise as to whether a party has the right to bring proceedings in court with respect to the challenge.
29-54
Among the criteria the President of the ICC Court may use when fixing the place for Emergency Arbitrator Proceedings may be
the place of residence of the prospective Emergency Arbitrator. Considerations of enforcement of the Emergency Arbitrator’s
Order may also play a role. This is something that the applicant would have to raise in the Application.
