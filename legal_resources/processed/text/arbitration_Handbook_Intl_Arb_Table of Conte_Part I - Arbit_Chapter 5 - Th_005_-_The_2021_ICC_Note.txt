At the same time, the Secretariat will send the arbitrators the latest version of its practice Note, i.e. currently the 2021 ICC
Note. 4  The 2021 ICC Note, which now comprises 40 pages, provides practical details on the conduct of the arbitration. It deals
in particular with:
 
-the various time limits under the Rules to be respected by the Tribunal;
 
-the expeditious and efficient conduct of the arbitration, including the techniques for controlling time and costs;
 
-dealing with manifestly unmeritorious claims and defenses;
 
-conduct of arbitration under the Expedited Procedure Provisions;
 
-the use of administrative secretaries;
 
-the advance on costs;
 
-the allocation and fixing of the arbitrator’s fees;
 
-the entitlement to personal and Tribunal expenses, and how to seek their reimbursement;
 
-VAT applicable to arbitrator’s fees;
 
-the replacement of arbitrators; and
 
-the closing of the proceedings and scrutiny of awards, including the ICC Award Checklist, additional administrative
services rendered by the ICC, including the ICC Hearing Center in Paris and International Center for ADR.
