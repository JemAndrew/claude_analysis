Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
20-F16
By an agreement in writing dated [date], the Claimant agreed to issue to the Defendant a credit token, namely an Xcard,
for the purpose of acquiring goods and services on credit, such credit being limited to £[..........]. A copy of the agreement
is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
The agreement was regulated by the Consumer Credit Act 1974 and this is a Consumer Credit Act claim.
3.
The following were provisions of the agreement:
 
(a)By clause 2(a) the Claimant was to send the Defendant a monthly statement of account showing the balance
currently due and the date on which that balance was payable to the Claimant (“the due date”).
 
(b)By clause 2(b) if the balance was not paid in full on the due date, the Defendant should pay interest on any
part outstanding after the due date at the Claimant’s published rate, such interest to accrue from day to day and be
compounded monthly.
 
(c)By clause 3(a) the monthly statement of account was to specify a minimum sum to be paid by the Defendant on
the due date.
 
(d)By clause 3(b) in default payment of the minimum sum on or before the due date, the entire balance due on that
date should become immediately repayable.
4.
Prior to [date], the Defendant made use of the credit token to acquire goods and services.
