Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently

20-F26
By an agreement in writing dated [date], (“the card agreement”) the Claimant agreed to issue to the First Defendant a
credit token, namely an Xcard, for the purpose of acquiring goods and services on credit. A copy of the card agreement
is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
The agreement was regulated by the Consumer Credit Act 1974 (“the CCA”).
3.
The Defendant was at all material times a dealer in computer equipment. By a written agreement dated [date] (“the dealer
agreement”), the Defendant agreed with the Claimant to accept the Xcard in payment for goods and services supplied by
him. A copy of the dealer agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
4.
On or about [date] the Defendant entered into an oral agreement with PQ (“the sale agreement”) whereby the Defendant
sold [identify the goods] (“the goods”) to PQ for a total price of £[..........].
5.
In discharge of the purchase price, PQ made use of the Xcard. In pursuance of the dealer agreement, the Defendant
subsequently sought reimbursement from the Claimant which was duly made.
6.
By s.12 of the Sale of Goods Act 1979, it was a term of the sale agreement that the Defendant had the right to sell the
goods and pass title therein to PQ.

6.
By s.17 of the Consumer Rights Act 2015, it was a term of the sale agreement that the Defendant had the right to sell the
goods and pass title therein to PQ.
7.
The use of the Xcard to make the purchase constituted a borrower-lender-supplier agreement to which s.75(1) of the Act
applied.
8.
In breach of the sale agreement, the Defendant did not have the right to sell the goods which were at all material times
owned by XY Limited. On [date] XY Limited, as it was entitled to do, demanded of PQ that he deliver up the goods to
them and PQ did so on that date.
9.
By a letter dated [date] (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim) PQ, as he was entitled to do, rescinded the sale
agreement and demanded the repayment of the said price of £[..........] by the Defendant. The Defendant has wrongfully
refused to repay the said sum or any part thereof.
10.
By a letter [date] (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim) pursuant to s.75(1) of the Act, PQ demanded payment of
the sum of £[..........] from the Claimant.
11.
On [date] the Claimant acknowledged the said rescission and paid to PQ the sum of £[..........].
12.
Pursuant to s.75(2) of the Act, the Defendant is liable to indemnify the Claimant.
The Claimant therefore claims:
 
(1)£[..........].
 
(2)Interest pursuant to s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984 at the rate of x% from [date] to the date hereof: £[..........]
and continuing at the rate of £[..........] per day to judgment herein.
