Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
25-F15
At all material times, the Defendant carried on the business of [identify business].
2.
By an agreement made [date] the Defendant agreed to sell to the Claimant [identify the goods] (“the goods”) for the price
of £[..........].
3.
The agreement was made partly orally and partly in writing. In so far as it was made orally, it was made between the
Claimant and the Defendant at [address], and in so far as it was in writing, it was contained in or evidenced by an order
form completed with details of the sale and signed by the Claimant (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim).
4.
It was an implied condition of the agreement that the Defendant had the right to sell the goods.
5.
In pursuance of the agreement, the Claimant duly paid to the Defendant the sum of £[..........] and the Defendant delivered
the goods to the Claimant.
6.
In breach of the said condition of the agreement, the Defendant did not have the right to sell the goods. At all material
times the true owner of the goods was PQ who had left the goods at the Defendant’s premises for the purposes of repair
and neither knew of nor consented to the sale of the goods by the Defendant to the Claimant.
7.
On [date] as he was entitled to do, PQ demanded delivery up of the goods from the Claimant and the Claimant had no
option but to comply with that demand.
