Article 5 of App.VI provides that in all matters concerning the Expedited Procedure Provisions not expressly provided for in
this Appendix, the court and the Tribunal shall act in the spirit of the ICC Rules and of App.VI. Unlike art.42, it does not
explicitly state that the court and the Tribunal shall make every effort to make sure that the award appears enforceable at law.
The practical use of this provision is questionable, as the Expedited Procedure Rules do not exclude the application of art.42.
