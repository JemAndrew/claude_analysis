Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
25-F7
The Claimant carries on business as a manufacturer of [identify products].
2.
By an oral agreement made between the Claimant and the Defendant on [date], the Claimant agreed to sell to the Defendant
[identify goods sold] (“the goods”) at a price of £[..........] (to include delivery at the Defendant’s premises at [address])
making a total purchase price of £[..........].
3.
The agreement was concluded in a telephone conversation between GH of the Claimant and JK of the Defendant and was
evidenced by an invoice dated [date] and sent by fax to the Defendant on [date] (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim).
4.
On [date] the Claimant duly delivered the goods at the Defendant’s premises but, in breach of the agreement, the Defendant
refused to accept delivery of the goods or to make payment for them.
5.
By reason of the Defendant’s breach of the agreement, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage:
Particulars
 
(a)Difference between the contract price and the market price on [date] namely £[..........].
 
(b)Cost of carriage of the goods to the Claimant’s premises at [address] following rejection by the Defendant £[..........].
 
(c)Storage of the goods pending resale £[..........].
The Claimant therefore claims:
 
(1)Damages.
 
(2)Interest pursuant to [s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981] [s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984] at the rate of x
% per annum from [date] to judgment.
