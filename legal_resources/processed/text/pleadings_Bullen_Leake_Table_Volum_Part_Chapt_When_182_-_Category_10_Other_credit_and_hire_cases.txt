When the “Consumer Credit Act Procedure” (“CCA Procedure”) applies
Category 10: Other credit and hire cases
20-52
All other credit and hire cases whether arising from a regulated or unregulated agreement are subject to the general procedural
rules which govern other categories of action save that, if the action is brought by a creditor or owner to enforce a regulated
agreement, the action must be brought in the county court under s.141(1). The particulars of claim need not be served with the
claim form and default judgment under Pt 12 is available.
