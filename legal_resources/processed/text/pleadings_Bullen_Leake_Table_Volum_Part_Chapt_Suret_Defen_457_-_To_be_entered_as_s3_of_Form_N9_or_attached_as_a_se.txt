Chapter 27 - Surety
Surety’s Defences
Defence under the Statute of Frauds 1677, s.4
To be entered as s.3 of Form N9 or attached as a separate document
1.
27-F6
The Defendant denies that the agreement of guarantee alleged in the Particulars of Claim was made in writing or evidenced
by a note or memorandum in writing or that any agreement, note or memorandum was signed by or on behalf of the
Defendant.
Or
1.
The document annexed to the Claim Form and relied upon by the Claimant as constituting an agreement of guarantee
between the Claimant and the Defendant is not signed by or on behalf of the Defendant.
2.
In the premises the alleged guarantee is unenforceable by reason of s.4 of the Statute of Frauds 1677 and the Defendant
is not liable to the Claimant in the sum claimed or any sum.
[Statement of truth]
