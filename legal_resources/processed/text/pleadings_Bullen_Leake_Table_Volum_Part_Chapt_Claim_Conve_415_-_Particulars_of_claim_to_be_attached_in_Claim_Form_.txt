Particulars of claim to be attached in Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
26-F2
By a written agreement dated [date], the Claimant sold the Defendant a [identify the vehicle] (“the vehicle”), under a
conditional sale contract so that the property in the vehicle was to remain the Claimant’s until such time as all the instalments
of the purchase price had been paid. A copy of the conditional sale agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
At a date unknown to the Claimant but in or about [date], the Defendant wrongfully and without the consent of the Claimant
purported to sell the vehicle to one Smith.
3.
Smith was a private purchaser acting in good faith and without notice of the conditional sale agreement and, pursuant to
s.27 of the Hire Purchase Act 1964, obtained good title to the vehicle.
4.
In selling the vehicle to Smith, the Defendant converted the vehicle to his own use.
5.
By reason of the conversion, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage.

(1)Damages.
 
(2)Interest under [s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984] [s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981].
