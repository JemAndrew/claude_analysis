1.
25-F2
The Claimant is a wholesale dealer in [identify merchandise], carrying on business in (among other places) warehouse
premises at [address].
2.
By a contract made [date] between the Claimant and the Defendant, the Claimant agreed to sell to the Defendant x tons of
[identify the goods] (“the goods”) at a price of £[..........] per ton ex-warehouse, making a total purchase price of £[..........].
3.
The contract was made in writing and contained in the Defendant’s order form dated [date] and the Claimant’s acceptance
of order dated [date]. Copies of those documents are annexed to these Particulars of Claim.
4.
On [date] the Claimant appropriated x tons of the goods to the contract and by an email of the same date informed the
Defendant that the goods were ready for collection from the warehouse (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim).
5.
Thereafter the Defendant failed or refused to collect the goods or any of part of them or to pay the said sum of £[..........]
or any part thereof.
The Claimant therefore claims:
 
(1)£[..........].
 
(2)£[..........] contractual interest alternatively interest under [s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984] [s.35A of the Senior
Courts Act 1981] accrued from [date when each sum became due] to the date of the issue of the claim and continuing
at the daily rate of £[..........] until judgment.
