The Claimant and the Defendant are both dealers in [identify products].
2.
By an agreement made orally in a telephone conversation between the Claimant and the Defendant on [date], the Defendant
agreed to sell to the Claimant [identify goods sold] (“the goods”) at a price of £[..........] per ton, to be delivered to the
Claimant’s premises at [address] as to [identify first consignment] on [date 1] and as to the balance on [date 2].

In breach of the agreement the Defendant failed to deliver either consignment of the goods either on the agreed dates or
at all.
4.
By reason of the Defendant’s breach of the agreement, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage.
