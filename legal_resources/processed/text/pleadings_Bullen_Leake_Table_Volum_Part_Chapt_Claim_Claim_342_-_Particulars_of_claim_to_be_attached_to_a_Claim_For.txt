Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
25-F12
The Claimant carries on business as [identify business] and the Defendant as [identify business].
2.
By an agreement made between the Claimant and the Defendant on [date] (“the sale agreement”), the Defendant agreed
to manufacture and sell to the Claimant [identify the goods] (“the goods”) to be delivered to the Claimant’s premises at
[address] on or before [date].
3.
The sale agreement was made in writing and contained in the Claimant’s order form dated [date] and the Defendant’s
acceptance of order dated [date]. Copies of those documents are annexed to these Particulars of Claim.
4.
At the time of the sale agreement the Defendant well knew that the Claimant was buying the goods for the purposes of
resale to [identify on-sales]. Further in a covering letter sent with the order (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim),
the Claimant informed the Defendant that he had obtained a very profitable purchase order for the goods from XY Limited.
5.
By an oral agreement made between the Claimant and XY Limited on [date] (“the resale agreement”), the Claimant agreed
to resell all the goods to XY Limited at a price of £[..........], to be delivered on [date].
6.
In breach of the sale agreement, the Defendant failed to deliver the said goods or any of it to the Claimant.
7.
By reason of the Defendant’s failure to deliver the goods to the Claimant, the Claimant was unable to fulfil the resale
agreement, the Defendant being the only source of supply of goods of that description. The Claimant thereby became in
breach of his contract with XY Limited.

compromised on terms that the Claimant paid XY Limited agreed damages of £[..........] and agreed costs of £[..........]. The
Claimant himself reasonably incurred costs of £[..........] in defending those proceedings.
9.
By reason of the Defendant’s breach of the agreement, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage.
