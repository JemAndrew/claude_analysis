Seller’s Defences
Denial of terms imposed by the Consumer Rights Act 2015
To be entered as s.3 of Form N9 or attached as a separate document
1.
25-F41
The Defendant admits that it entered into the oral agreement with the Claimant whereby the Defendant agreed to sell and
deliver the goods referred to in the Particulars of Claim.
2.
The Defendant denies that he sold the goods to the Claimant in the course of a business. In the circumstances he was not
a trader for the purposes of the Consumer Rights Act 2015 and the sale contract was not one to which that Act applied.
3.
Consequently the agreement was not subject to the terms imported into consumer contracts by the 2015 Act whether as
alleged in the Particulars of Claim or at all.
