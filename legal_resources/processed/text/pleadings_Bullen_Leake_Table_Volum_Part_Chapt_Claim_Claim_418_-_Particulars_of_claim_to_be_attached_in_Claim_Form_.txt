Claim for conversion by destruction of the goods
Particulars of claim to be attached in Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
26-F5
The Claimant is the owner of a consignment of x tons of [identify material] valued at £[..........].
2.
At a date unknown to the Claimant, the consignment was delivered to the Defendant at their manufacturing premises
at [address] where, without the Claimant’s knowledge or consent, it used the Claimant’s material in the manufacture of
[identify product].
3.
In the premises the Defendant converted the said material to its own use.
4.
By reason of the conversion, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage, namely £[..........], the value of the material.
5.
The Claimant is entitled to interest on the value of the converted goods from the date of conversion at such rate as the
Court deems just pursuant to [s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984] [s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981].
The Claimant therefore claims:
 
(1)Damages for conversion.
 
(2)Interest under [s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984] [s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981].
