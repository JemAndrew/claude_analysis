Section 7 - Legal Representatives—Costs and Litigation Funding
Section 7A - Litigation Funding after 1 April 2013
Conditional Fee Agreements Order 2013
5.— Amount of success fee in specified proceedings
7A-19
(1) In relation to the proceedings specified in article 4, the percentage prescribed for the purposes of section 58(4B)
(c) of the Act is—
(a) in proceedings at first instance, 25%; and
(b) in all other proceedings, 100%.
(2) The descriptions of damages specified for the purposes of section 58(4B)(d) of the Act are—
(a) general damages for pain, suffering, and loss of amenity; and
(b) damages for pecuniary loss, other than future pecuniary loss,
net of any sums recoverable by the Compensation Recovery Unit of the Department for Work and Pensions.
Risk assessment
7A-20
It is not appropriate for a legal representative to agree with the client that the success fee will be 25% of the damages or 100%
of the base costs (limited to 25% of the damages) without first carrying out an assessment of the risk: Herbert v HH Law Ltd
[2018] EWHC 580 (QB). Affirmed on appeal: [2019] EWCA Civ 527.
