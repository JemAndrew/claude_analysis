Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
20-F2
By a hire-purchase agreement in writing dated [date], the Claimant let to the Defendant on hire-purchase a [identify the
goods] (“the goods”). A copy of the agreement is annexed to these Particulars of Claim.
2.
The cash price of the goods was £[..........], the deposit was £[..........]and the balance of £[..........] together with interest
charges of £[..........] was payable by x equal consecutive monthly instalments of £[..........]. The total price of the goods
was £[..........].
3.
By clause 3 of the agreement, the Defendant agreed to pay interest of x% over PQ Bank Plc base rate on all overdue sums.
4.
By clause 10 of the agreement, if the Defendant defaulted upon the payment of any instalment, time for payment being of
the essence of the agreement, the Claimant could forthwith determine the agreement and the hiring and repossess the goods.
5.
In pursuance of the agreement, the Defendant paid the deposit and x monthly instalments, making a total payment of
£[..........].
6.
In breach of the agreement the Defendant failed to pay the instalment due [date] or any subsequent instalment. At [date]
the instalments were £[..........] in arrear. The further sum of £[..........] was due by way of contractual interest.

7.
By a letter dated [date] (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim), the Claimant as it was entitled to do, terminated the
agreement and the hiring and demanded delivery up of the goods. Notwithstanding that demand, the Defendant has failed
or refused to deliver them up.

At the date of this claim, the value of the goods is £[..........].
The Claimant therefore claims:
 
(1)Delivery up of the goods or payment of £[..........] its value.
 
(2)£[..........] arrears of instalments.
 
(3)£[..........] contractual interest accrued from [date when each sum became due] to the date of the issue of the claim
and continuing at the daily rate of £[..........] until judgment.
[or, where appropriate]

an annual rate of x% from [date when each sum became due] to the date of the issue of the claim, and continuing at
the daily rate of £[..........] until judgment.
[and, for contracts made after 6 August 2002]
 
(4)Sum payable under s.5A of the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998: £[..........].
[followed by]
