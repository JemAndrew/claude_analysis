The Claimant carries on business as an auctioneer of motor vehicles. The Defendant is a motor trader.
2.
By an oral agreement made [date] between the Claimant and the Defendant, the Defendant instructed the Claimant to sell
by auction 120 assorted motor vehicles on [date].
3.
The agreement was made in a telephone conversation between LM of the Claimant and PQ of the Defendant on [date].
4.
It was a necessarily implied condition of the agreement that, in the event of the Defendant cancelling the auction, the
Defendant should fully reimburse the Claimant for work done and expenses incurred in and about the preparations for
the auction.
5.
In pursuance of the agreement, on [date] the Defendant delivered the vehicles to the Claimant’s premises at [address] and
the Claimant made all necessary arrangements for the storage, cleaning and security of the vehicles and for advertising
the auction.
6.
On [date], the Defendant withdrew the vehicles from the auction. On [date] the Defendant removed the vehicles from the
Claimant’s premises.
7.
In the premises the Claimant is entitled to be reimbursed the work done and expenses incurred in preparation for the auction.
