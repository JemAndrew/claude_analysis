Particulars of claim to be included in the Claim Form N1 or served subsequently

20-F33
By a written agreement dated [date] made between the Claimant and XY Limited, (“the supply agreement”) XY Limited
agreed to supply and install [identify the goods] (“the goods”) at [address] on [date] for the sum of £[..........]. A copy of
the agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
There were express terms of the supply agreement that [set out relevant terms as to performance and quality].
3.
By a written agreement dated [date], (“the loan agreement”) the Defendant agreed to advance the sum of £[..........] to the
Claimant for the purpose of paying the sums due under the supply agreement. A copy of the loan agreement is annexed
to this Claim Form.
4.
The loan agreement was a borrower-lender-supplier agreement within s.75 of the Consumer Credit Act 1974 (“the CCA”).
Alternatively
4.
In relation to the supply agreement, the loan agreement was a linked credit agreement for the purposes of s.75A of the
Consumer Credit Act 1974 (“the CCA”).
5.
XY Limited purported to carry out the work pursuant to the supply contract and the Claimant paid the said price of £[..........]:
 
(a)as to £[..........] in cash

(a)XY Limited cannot be traced [particulars]

(1)Damages.

[Statement of truth]
Note
CCA s.75A, which applies to agreements for credit where the cash price of the goods or services is between £30,000 and £60,260
has slightly different criteria for its application (linked agreements not being exactly equivalent to borrower-lender-supplier
agreements as in s.75). Also a s.75A claim cannot be pursued at all unless at least one of the four conditions set out in s.75A(2)
is met: the alternative para.8 above sets out the conditions.
