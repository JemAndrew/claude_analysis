Defence
1.
The Defendant admits that it entered into the written contract with the Claimant annexed to the Claim Form whereby the
Defendant agreed to sell and deliver the goods referred to in the Particulars of Claim.
2.
The Defendant denies that it failed to deliver the goods referred to in the Particulars of Claim [or any part thereof]. The
Defendant tendered delivery of the goods at the Claimant’s premises at [address] on [date] in accordance with the contract.
Wrongfully and in breach of the contract, the Claimant refused to accept delivery of the goods or to pay the purchase price.
