Part I - Arbitration Rules
Chapter 5 - The Arbitral Proceedings
“Opting In” to the Expedited Procedure Rules
Article 30(1)
“By agreeing to arbitration under the Rules, the parties agree that this Article 30 and the Expedited Procedure Rules set
forth in Appendix VI (collectively the “Expedited Procedure Provisions”) shall take precedence over any contrary terms
of the arbitration agreement.”
