Rule 44_1
44.1 Scope of this Part 1
7B-8+
This Part contains general rules about costs, entitlement to costs and orders in respect of pro bono representation.
(The definitions contained in Part 43 are relevant to this Part.)
Effect of rule
7B-8. 1+ For definitions, see r.43.2(1) at para.7B-2+.
