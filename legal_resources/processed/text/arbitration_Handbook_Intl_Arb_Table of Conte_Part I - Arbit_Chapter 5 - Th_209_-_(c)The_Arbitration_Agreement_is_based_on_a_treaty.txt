(c)—The Arbitration Agreement is based on a treaty
29-128
Article 29(6)(c) was added with the 2021 Rules in order to state expressly that it is considered inappropriate to have Emergency
Arbitration Proceedings in treaty-based cases. 630  The new language does not reflect any substantive change as there were
doubts as to whether parties to an investment arbitration could be considered signatories within the meaning of art.29(5) and
the Court’s practice was to not include treaty-based arbitrations. 631
