Claim on a bond with special condition (not to engage in competing business)
1.
11-E2
On [date], the Defendant executed a bond in the sum of £5,000 (“the Bond”) conditioned void provided that AB shall not
whilst in the employment of the Claimant or for a period of [years/months] following the termination of his employment
for any cause carry on or be engaged in the business of [specify business] within [specify geographical extent] except
on behalf of or with the express written consent of the Claimant. A copy of the Bond is attached to these Particulars of
Claim marked “A”.
2.
On [date], AB took up employment with XY Ltd, which was engaged in the business of [..........] at [employer/address].
3.
By reason of the matters set out above, the Claimant is entitled to and claims £5,000 as liquidated damages.
4.
Further, the Claimant is entitled to and claims interest pursuant to s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981 at the rate of 8
per cent per annum on the sum of £5,000 from [date], amounting to £[..........] at [date of claim] and continuing at the
daily rate of £[..........].
AND the Claimant claims:
 
(1)£5,000;
 
(2)Interest pursuant to s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981, under paragraph 4 above.
