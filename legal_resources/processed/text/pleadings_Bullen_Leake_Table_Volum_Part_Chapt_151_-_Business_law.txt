Business law
19-01
In an earlier edition of this work, it was decided to group a number of subjects together under the general heading “Business
Law”. Business law is not, of course, a term of art and many other sections of this work will contain material relevant to
business disputes. What Part D aims to achieve is a handy reference for those dealing with the everyday problems of commercial
business, particularly the sale or supply of goods, digital content and services whether for outright payment or on some form
of credit or lease.
