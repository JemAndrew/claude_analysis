Defence alleging discharge by payment to, or deemed to have been to, true owner
1.
10-E22
It is admitted that the Defendant drew and delivered to the Claimant the cheque drawn upon ABC Bank Plc which is
sued upon.
2.
Thereafter, the cheque was presented by EFG Bank Plc to ABC Bank Plc who paid it to EFG Bank for the account of their
customer. That payment was made in good faith and without negligence.
3.
In the circumstances if (which the Defendant can neither admit nor deny) payment was not made to the account of the
Claimant, nonetheless the Defendant was discharged as though payment had been made to the Claimant.
