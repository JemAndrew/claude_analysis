Exclusion of Emergency Arbitrator’s Liability
29-103
Article 41 of the Rules also expressly covers the Emergency Arbitrator, and thus excludes him or her from any liability “to any
person for any act or omission in connection with the arbitration”.
29-104
Although no specific confidentiality obligation is imposed on the Emergency Arbitrator by the Emergency Arbitrator Provisions,
the latter is expected to keep the information relating to the Emergency Arbitrator Proceedings confidential, in the same way
an arbitrator is expected to keep the information relating to the arbitration confidential.
