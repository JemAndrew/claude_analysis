Proof of authority requested by the Tribunal
17-10
As soon as the Tribunal has received the file from the Secretariat pursuant to art.16, and during anytime thereafter, it may request
proof of authority from the parties’ representatives. In practice, it is mostly when the Terms of Reference are being signed that
Tribunals request a power of attorney. The Terms of Reference are a supplemental agreement among the parties and the Tribunal
with respect to the arbitration and it is therefore important that the signatories be authorized. 20  As a matter of equal treatment
of both parties and to avoid any suspicion of bias, it is advisable to ask for a power from all participants, even if to the Tribunal
only the representation of one of the parties may appear questionable. 21
