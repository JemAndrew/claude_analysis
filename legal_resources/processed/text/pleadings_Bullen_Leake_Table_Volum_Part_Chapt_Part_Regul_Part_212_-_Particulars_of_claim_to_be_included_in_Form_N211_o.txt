Note
The question whether or not the right of the borrower to rescind the supply agreement may give him the right under CCA 1974,
s.75(1) to rescind the credit agreement used to be highly controversial. The controversy was resolved by the Supreme Court in
Durkin v DSG Retail Ltd [2014] UKSC 21 which held that, while, on is true construction, CCA 1974 s.75(1) did not give the
borrower the right to rescind the credit agreement, the common law would imply into the credit agreement a term that it could
not survive the rescission of the supply contract. Thus rescission of the supply contract would give the right to rescind the credit
agreement not under s.75(1) but by the working out of a necessarily implied contractual term.
