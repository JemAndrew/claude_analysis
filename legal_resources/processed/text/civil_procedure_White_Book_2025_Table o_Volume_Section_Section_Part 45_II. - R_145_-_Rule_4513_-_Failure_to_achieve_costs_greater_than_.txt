45.13— Failure to achieve costs greater than fixed recoverable costs 1
7B-28+
(1) This rule applies where—
(a) costs are assessed in accordance with rule 45.12(2); and
(b) the court assesses the costs (excluding any VAT) as being an amount which is less than 20% greater than
the amount of the fixed recoverable costs.
(2) The court must order the defendant to pay to the claimant the lesser of—
(a) the fixed recoverable costs; and
(b) the assessed costs.
Effect of rule
7B-28.
1+
This rule applies in relation to a “pre-commencement funding arrangement”; see para.7B-19+, and paras 48.0.1 to 48.0.4
