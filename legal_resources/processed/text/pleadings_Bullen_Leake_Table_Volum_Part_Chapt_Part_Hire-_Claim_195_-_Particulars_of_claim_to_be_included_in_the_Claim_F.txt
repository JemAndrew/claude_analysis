Particulars of claim to be included in the Claim Form N1 or served with it
1.
20-F10
By a hire-purchase agreement in writing dated [date], the Claimant let to the Defendant on hire-purchase a [identify the
goods] (“the goods”). A copy of the agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
The agreement was regulated by the Consumer Credit Act 1974 (“the CCA”) and this is a Consumer Credit Act claim
subject to the Consumer Credit Act procedure.
3.
The cash price of the goods was £[..........], the deposit was £[..........] and the Credit of £[..........] with charges of £[..........]
was payable by x equal consecutive monthly instalments of £[..........]. The total purchase price was £[..........]. The APR
was x%.
4.
Under the CCA s 66A, the Defendant had a right to withdraw from the agreement by giving notice pursuant to s 66A(2).
5.
The following are the particulars required by the Civil Procedure Rules, PD16.6.2:
 
(1)the date of the agreement: [date]
 
(2)the parties to the agreement: the Claimant and the Defendant
 
(3)the number or other identification of the agreement: [number]
 
(4)where the Claimant was not one of the original parties to the agreement, the means by which the rights and duties
of the lender passed to him: not applicable
