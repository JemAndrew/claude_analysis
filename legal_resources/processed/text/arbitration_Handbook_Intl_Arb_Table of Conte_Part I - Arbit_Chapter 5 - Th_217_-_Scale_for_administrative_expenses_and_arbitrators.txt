Appendix III of the ICC Rules contains the scales, or arbitrator’s fees concerning the expedited procedures and, to which
art.4(2) of App.VI refers. Unlike the ICC’s administrative fees under the Expedited Procedure Provisions, the arbitrators’ fees
are reduced by 20 per cent compared to the rates in standard ICC arbitration proceedings. 649

30-17
The wisdom of the decision to reduce by some 20 per cent the arbitrators’ base fees does not seem evident. Admittedly, arbitrators
in expedited proceedings will have somewhat less work, since they are dispensed of drafting Terms of Reference, and may not
have to hear witnesses or even hold hearings. 651  At the same time, they will have to complete their work in a much shorter
period of time, and make themselves available at shorter intervals, which for busy practitioners (which most arbitrators normally
are) also comes at a price. 652
