Section 7 - Legal Representatives—Costs and Litigation Funding
Section 7A - Litigation Funding after 1 April 2013
Conditional Fee Agreements Order 2013
2. Agreements providing for a success fee
7A-16
All proceedings which, under section 58 of the Act, can be the subject of an enforceable conditional fee agreement, except
proceedings under section 82 of the Environmental Protection Act 1990, are proceedings specified for the purpose of
section 58(4)(a) of the Act.
