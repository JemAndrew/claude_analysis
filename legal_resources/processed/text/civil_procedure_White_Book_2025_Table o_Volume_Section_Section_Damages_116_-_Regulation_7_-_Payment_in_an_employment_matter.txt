Section 7A - Litigation Funding after 1 April 2013
Damages-Based Agreements Regulations 2013
Regulation 7
7. Payment in an employment matter
7A-33
In an employment matter, a damages-based agreement must not provide for a payment above an amount which, including
VAT, is equal to 35% of the sums ultimately recovered by the client in the claim or proceedings.
Editorial note
7A-33. 1 For Employment claims the upper limit on payment to the representatives must not exceed 35% (inclusive of VAT) of the sums
ultimately recovered by the client in the claim or proceedings.
