Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently

25-F28
The Claimant carries on business as an auctioneer of motor vehicles. The Defendant is a motor trader.
2.
By an oral agreement made [date] between the Claimant and the Defendant, the Defendant instructed the Claimant to sell
by auction [identify vehicle] (“the vehicle”).
3.
The agreement was made by LM of the Claimant and PQ of the Defendant at a meeting on [date] at the Claimant’s premises
at [address] and was evidenced by a handwritten note signed by PQ and handed to LM (copy annexed to these Particulars
of Claim).
4.
There were necessarily implied conditions of the agreement that:
 
(a)the Defendant had the right to sell the vehicle;
 
(b)the Defendant should indemnify the Claimant against any loss sustained by reason of the sale.
5.
In pursuance of the agreement on [date] the Claimant sold the vehicle at auction for £[..........] and delivered it to the
purchaser.
6.
In breach of the said conditions, the Defendant did not have the right to sell the vehicle. At all times, the vehicle belonged
to XY Limited who had left it with the Defendant for repair. On [date] XY Limited commenced an action against the
Defendant in the [..........] Court alleging that the Claimant had unlawfully converted the vehicle. On [date] XY Limited
obtained summary judgment against the Claimant for £[..........] together with interest of £[..........] and costs of £[..........],
making a total of £[..........]. The Claimant itself incurred legal costs of £[..........] in respect of the proceedings.
