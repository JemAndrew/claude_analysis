Remedy claimed and grounds on which claim is
made
The Claimant claims:
1.
An interim anti-suit injunction pursuant to section 37(1) of the Senior Courts Act 1981, restraining the Defendant, whether
by itself, its directors, officers, employees, agents or otherwise, from commencing and further pursuing, or taking any
further steps in, or procuring or assisting in the pursuit of or the taking of steps in: (a) its proceedings before the High Court
of [X], [Country] under the reference case No. [--] (“the [Country] Proceedings”), save for the purposes of dismissing,
withdrawing and/or otherwise discontinuing the said [Country] Proceedings against the Claimant; and (b) any other
proceedings brought in breach of the terms of clause [e.g. 28] of the [e.g. Share Sale and Purchase Agreement] dated [date]
between the Claimant and the Defendant (“the [e.g. SSPA]”).
2.
An interim declaration that the Defendant is obliged to arbitrate all disputes arising out of the SSPA pursuant to the terms
of clause [e.g. 28] of the SSPA.
3.
Permission to serve this claim form, the response pack (consisting of Forms N8B and N15), the Claimant’s evidence in
support of its application, any Order of this Court, and any other documents requiring service on the Defendant under
the Civil Procedure Rules in regard to this claim on the Defendant in [the Country] at the address specified herein. The
Claimant founds this application on (i) CPR 62.5(1)(c) and/or (ii) CPR 6.36 (pursuant to paragraph 3.1(6)(c) of Practice
Direction 6B to the CPR).
4.
Such further or other relief as may be appropriate.
5.
The costs of these proceedings.
By reason of the grounds set out in detail in the witness statement dated [date] of [Witness] in support of this claim attached
hereto.
