Particulars of claim to be attached in Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
26-F3
The Claimant is the owner of the goods listed in the schedule annexed to this Claim Form, (“the goods”).
2.
The goods were stolen from the Claimant on [date].
3.
After the theft but at a date unknown to the Claimant, the goods came into the possession of the Defendant.
4.
On or about [date], the Defendant delivered the goods to a person whose identity the Claimant is unable to establish.
5.
In storing the Claimant’s property and thereafter delivering it to a stranger, the Defendant converted the Claimant’s goods
to his own use.
6.
By reason of the conversion, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage. The value of the goods was £[..........].
7.
The Claimant is entitled to interest from the date of conversion pursuant to [s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984] [s.35A
of the Senior Courts Act 1981].
The Claimant therefore claims:
 
(1)Damages.
 
(2)Interest under [s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984] [s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981].
