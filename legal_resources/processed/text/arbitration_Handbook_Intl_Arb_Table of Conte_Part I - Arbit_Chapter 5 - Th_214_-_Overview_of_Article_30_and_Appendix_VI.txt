Overview of Article 30 and Appendix VI
30-7
Article 30 and App.VI set out the Expedited Procedures Provisions. While art.30 defines the basic principles regarding the
applicability of the Expedited Procedure Provisions, the Expedited Procedure Rules in App.VI provide through five different
articles detailed substantial rules governing the ICC expedited procedures. 644  Their main features are to be found in arts 2–4
of App.VI. 645  These provisions should be read in the light of Part VIII of the 2021 ICC Note.
30-8
Article 30(1) states that by agreeing to the ICC Rules, parties agree that the Expedited Procedure Provisions will take precedence
over any contrary terms of the arbitration agreement. The use of an appendix to the Rules gives better flexibility for modifying
certain of its provisions without modifying the principles laid out in the Rules themselves. For instance, the threshold amount
for which the Expedited Procedure Provisions apply is defined in App.VI may more easily be revised than if it were stated in
art.30 of the Rules. The cost schedule in App.III of the Rules is regularly revised, without a corresponding change in the Rules.
