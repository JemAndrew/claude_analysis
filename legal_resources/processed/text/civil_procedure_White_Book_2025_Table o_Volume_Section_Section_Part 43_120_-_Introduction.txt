Section 7 - Legal Representatives—Costs and Litigation Funding
Section 7B - CPR 43 to 45 [Before 1 April 2013]
Part 43 [Before 1 April 2013] Scope of Costs Rules and Definitions
 
Related Practice Directions
Editorial introduction
7B-0. 1+ As the text of r.43.1 indicates, this Part contains definitions and interpretation of certain matters set out in the rules about costs
contained in Parts 44 to 48. In the period since this Part was first brought into effect on 26 April 1999, some of the definitions
in r.43.2 have been amended and definitions of new terms have been added.
Post-April 2013 application
7B-0. 2+ With effect from 1 April 2013, by r.15 of the Civil Procedure (Amendment) Rules 2013 (SI 2013/262), Part 43 was revoked and,
by r.16, Pts 44 to 48 as they stood before that date were substituted by Pts 44 to 48 as set out in the Schedule to that statutory
instrument. By CPR Update 60, the Costs Practice Direction was revoked and replaced by several new practice directions.
However, by operation of transitional and savings provisions, various provisions in the Parts revoked (including Pt 43) and
related practice directions continue to have effect for certain purposes.
In Pt 48 as substituted by the Civil Procedure (Amendment) Rules 2013 (SI 2013/262), brought into effect on 1 April 2013,
r.48.1(1) states that the provisions “relating to funding arrangements” in Pt 43 (and in Pts 45 to 48), and the attendant provisions
of the Costs Practice Direction, will apply in relation to “a pre-commencement funding arrangement” (as defined in r.48.2) as
they were in force immediately before 1 April 2013, with such modifications (if any) as may be made by a practice direction
on or after that date.
For detailed explanation, see paras 48.0.1 to 48.0.4 “Continuing Application of CPR Parts 43 to 48 and Costs Practice Direction
as in force immediately before 1 April 2013”.
Rules as in force before 1 April 2013, in Pt 43 “relating to funding arrangements” and which apply in relation to “a pre-
commencement funding arrangement” include the following: sub-paras (a), (k), (l), (m), (n) and (o) of r.43.2(1), and paras (3)
and (4) of r.43.2.
