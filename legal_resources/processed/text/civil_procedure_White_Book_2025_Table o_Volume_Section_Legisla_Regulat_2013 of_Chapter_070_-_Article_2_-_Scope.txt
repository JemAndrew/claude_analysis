Section 5 - International Jurisdiction and Enforcement of Judgments
Part - Legislation
Regulation (EU) No.606/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 12 June 2013 on mutual recognition of
protection measures in civil matters
Chapter I Subject Matter, Scope and Definitions
Article 2 Scope
5-155+
1. This Regulation applies where the recognition or enforcement of a protection measure in civil matters ordered by
an issuing authority is sought in England and Wales or Northern Ireland.
2. [Omitted]
3. This Regulation shall not apply to protection measures falling within the scope of Regulation (EC) No 2201/2003
as it has effect in EU law or as it has effect in the law of England and Wales and Northern Ireland.
Note
