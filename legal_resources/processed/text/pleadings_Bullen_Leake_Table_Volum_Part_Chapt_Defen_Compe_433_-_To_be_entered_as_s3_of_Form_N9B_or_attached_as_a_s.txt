1.
26-F20
The Defendant is unable to admit or deny whether the Claimant is the owner of the goods referred to in paragraph 1 of the
Particulars of Claim and requires the Claimant to prove the same.
2.
By a letter dated [date], a copy of which is annexed to this Defence, solicitors acting for Y Limited asserted that Y Limited
was the owner of the goods and demanded the delivery up of them to Y Limited. The Defendant is unable to determine
the competing claims of the Claimant and Y Limited.
3.
The Defendant makes no claim to an interest in the goods, nor a right to possession of them.
4.
The Defendant is not colluding with Y Limited in relation to the property and is willing to transfer the property to such
person as the Court may direct.
