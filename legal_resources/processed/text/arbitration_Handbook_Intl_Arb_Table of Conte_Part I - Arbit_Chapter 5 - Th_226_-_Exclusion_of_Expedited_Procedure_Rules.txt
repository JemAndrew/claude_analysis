Exclusion of Expedited Procedure Rules
30-30
Article 30(3) covers three specific situations where the application of the Expedited Procedure Provisions is expressly excluded.
Article 30(1) is therefore always to be read in conjunction with art.30(2). The Expedited Procedure Provisions apply only
provided they are not or have not been excluded pursuant to art.30(2). The method used in art.30 is identical for the one used
in 2012 when the new art.29 and App.V dealing with the Emergency Arbitrator Rules were introduced.
30-31
In practice, the Secretariat faced with a variety of different scenarios: although the Expedited Procedure Provisions would seem
to apply in principle, either the claimant or the respondent does not want them to apply; or, the opposite situation, the Expedited
Procedure Provisions do not apply in principle but either the claimant or the respondent argue that they do or should apply.
This may lead to some uncertainty at the start of the arbitration, and become the cause of new delays prior to the constitution
of the arbitral tribunal.
