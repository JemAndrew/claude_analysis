Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
25-F30
The Claimant is a motor trader and the Defendant carries on business as an auctioneer of [identify nature of business].
2.
By an agreement made [date] between the Claimant and the Defendant, the Defendant instructed the Claimant to sell by
auction [identify the goods] (“the goods”).
3.
The agreement was made in writing in the Defendant’s standard form of contract entered with details of the Claimant’s
instructions and signed by the Claimant. A copy of the agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
4.
The following were express conditions of the agreement:
 
(a)the sale must be subject to a reserve of £[..........] and the existence of a reserve communicated to the bidders;
 
(b)the bidders must be notified that [state fact about the goods which the auctioneer agreed to communicate];
 
(c)the sale must be conducted on terms that the all conditions and warranties as to the fitness or quality of the car
were excluded.
5.
In breach of the agreement, in an advertisement in [identify publication], the Defendant advertised the sale and described
the goods as [set out relevant part of the advertisement].
6.
In further breach of the agreement at the auction on [date], the Defendant failed to notify the existence of any reserve and
failed to impose on bidders an exclusion of all conditions and warranties as to the fitness or quality of the goods.
