By a contract made orally on [..........] between the Claimant and the Defendant the Defendant agreed to buy and the
Claimant agreed to sell a motor car registration number [..........] for a price of £2,000.
2.
The Claimant delivered that motor car to the Defendant.
3.
In payment of the price the Defendant drew and delivered to the Claimant a cheque dated [date] drawn upon the XY branch
of the ABC Bank Plc for the sum of £2,000.
4.
Upon due presentation of the cheque for payment it was dishonoured [and returned marked [refer to drawer/payment
countermanded, etc.].

marked (B). or

countermanded by the Defendant] notice of dishonour was dispensed with.

The cheque remains unpaid and the Defendant has not paid the price for the motor car.
7.

(1)payment of the sum of £2,000, together with
