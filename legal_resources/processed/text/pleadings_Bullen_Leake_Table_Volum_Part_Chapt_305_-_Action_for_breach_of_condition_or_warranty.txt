Action for breach of condition or warranty
25-21
The law in this area was greatly amended during the 1990s with the reworking of SGA ss.13 to 15 of the Act and the addition
of, SGA Pt 5A, which contained new and drastic remedies for the consumer. Most of these provisions were re-enacted into the
CRA and replace those of the SGA in consumer sales made on or after 1 October 2015.
