Part 2 Cost Protection
7. Cost protection in relation to legal help, help with family mediation or family help (lower)
7D-110+
Cost protection applies where a legally aided party receives legal help, help with family mediation or family help (lower)
in relation to proceedings (other than family proceedings) and later receives, in respect of the same proceedings—
(a) family help (higher); or
(b) legal representation.
