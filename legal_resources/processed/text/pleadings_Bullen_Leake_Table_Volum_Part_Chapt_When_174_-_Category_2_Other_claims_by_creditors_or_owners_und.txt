Category 2: Other claims by creditors or owners under unregulated hire-purchase and conditional sale agreements
(status of defendant irrelevant)
20-44
These proceedings may be started either in the High Court or the County Court on ordinary Part 7 principles. CCA Procedure
does not apply.
There are two sub-categories:
 
(a)claims against corporate and non-corporate defendants which do not involve the delivery of goods;
 
(b)claims against corporate defendants involving the delivery of goods.
The former sub-category is governed by CPR PD 16.6.2. The contents of the Claim Form are again strictly regulated and the
form must contain the particulars set out in CPR PD 16.6.2 (in the right order).
The second sub-category appears not to be the subject of any specific procedure and the ordinary pleading rules of the CPR
may be adopted.
Note that these rules do not apply to credit-sale agreements nor do they apply to any kind of hire agreement.
