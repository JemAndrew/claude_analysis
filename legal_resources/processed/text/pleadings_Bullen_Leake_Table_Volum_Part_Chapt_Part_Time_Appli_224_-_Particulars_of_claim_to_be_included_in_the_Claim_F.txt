20-F38
By a written agreement dated [date], the Defendant let to the Claimant on hire-purchase [identify the goods] (“the goods”).
A copy of the agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
The agreement was regulated by the Consumer Credit Act 1974 (“the CCA”) and this is a Consumer Credit Act claim
subject to the Consumer Credit Act procedure.
3.
The cash price of the goods was £[..........], the deposit was £[..........] and the balance of £[..........] with charges of £[..........]
was payable by [..........] equal consecutive monthly instalments of £[..........]. The total purchase price was £[..........].
4.
In pursuance of the agreement, the Claimant paid the deposit and instalments, totalling £[..........].
5.
Having become unemployed, the Claimant failed to pay the instalment due on [date] and has not paid any instalment
thereafter. The Claimant accepts that at the date of this claim, the instalments are £[..........] in arrear.
6.
By a notice dated [date] (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim), the Defendant required the Claimant to remedy the
breach and gave notice that in default of such remedy, the agreement and the hiring would terminate.

6.
On [date] the Defendant served on the Claimant a notice of arrears under s.[88B][88C] of the CCA and on [date] the
Claimant served on the Defendant a notice under s.129A of the Act. Copies of both notices are attached to this Claim. The
Defendant has failed to respond to the Claimant’s notice within 14 days or at all.
7.
The Claimant seeks a time order under s.129 of the CCA.
8.
Particulars required by CPR, Pt 7, PD 7B, para.7.3:
 
(1)the date of the agreement: [date]
 
(2)the parties to the agreement: the Claimant and the Defendant
 
(3)the number or other means of identifying the agreement: [number]
 
(4)details of any sureties: none
 
(5)if the Defendant is not one of the original parties to the agreement then the name of the original party to the
agreement: not applicable
 
(6)details of the notice by the lender or owner giving rise to the claim for the time order: notice of [date] referred
to in paragraph 6 above.
 
(7)the total unpaid balance the Claimant admits is due under the agreement: £[..........] and
 
(a)the amount of any arrears: £[..........] and
 
(b)the amount and frequency of the payments specified in the agreement: £[..........] per month.
 
(8)the Claimant’s proposals for payment of any arrears and of future instalments together with details of his means:
see following paragraphs.
9.
The Claimant’s proposal is that he should, with effect from [date], pay the current instalments of rent together with £[..........]
off the arrears, making a total monthly payment of £[..........].
10.
The means of the Claimant are as follows [fill in details of means]. The Claimant therefore claims such time order as the
Court may think just.
