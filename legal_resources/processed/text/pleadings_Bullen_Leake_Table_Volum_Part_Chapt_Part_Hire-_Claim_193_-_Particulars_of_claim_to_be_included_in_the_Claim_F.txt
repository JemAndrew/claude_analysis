By a hire-purchase agreement in writing dated [date], the Claimant let to the Defendant on hire-purchase a [identify the
goods] (“the goods”). A copy of the agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
The agreement was regulated by the Consumer Credit Act 1974 (“the CCA”) and this is a Consumer Credit Act claim
subject to the Consumer Credit Act procedure.
3.
The cash price of the goods was £[..........], the deposit was £[..........] and the balance of £[..........] with charges of £[..........]
was payable by x equal consecutive monthly instalments of £[..........]. The total purchase price was £[..........].
4.

5.
By clause 7 of the agreement, the Defendant agreed to maintain the car in a careful and proper manner, to ensure its regular
servicing and to repair at his own expense any damage to the car, however caused.
