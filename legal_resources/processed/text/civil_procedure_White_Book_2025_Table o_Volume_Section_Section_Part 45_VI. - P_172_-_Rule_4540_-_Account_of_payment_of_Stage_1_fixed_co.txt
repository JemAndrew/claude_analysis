Rule 45_40
45.40 Account of payment of Stage 1 fixed costs 1
7B-55+
Where a claim no longer continues under the RTA Protocol the court will, when making any order as to costs including
an order for fixed recoverable costs under Section II of this Part, take into account the Stage 1 fixed costs together with
any success fee on those costs that have been paid by the defendant.
Effect of rule
7B-55.
1+
For circumstances in which, after 1 April 2013, additional costs liability may be recovered in the form of a success fee, see
commentary following r.45.31 above. There is no right to repayment of the Stage 1 fixed costs if the claim is not then pursued:
J C and A Solicitors Ltd v Iqbal [2017] EWCA Civ 355; [2017] 3 Costs L.O. 377 (CA).
