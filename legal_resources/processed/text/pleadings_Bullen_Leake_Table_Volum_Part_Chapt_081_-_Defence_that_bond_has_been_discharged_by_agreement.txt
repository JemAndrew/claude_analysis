Defence that bond has been discharged by agreement or material alteration
1.
11-E10
It is admitted that the Defendant executed the bond alleged in the Particulars of Claim (“the Bond”).
2.
The cause of action alleged in the Particulars of Claim was released by deed made between the Claimant and the Defendant
dated [date].
3.
Further or alternatively, after the Bond was executed and whilst it was in the possession or custody of the Claimant it was
materially altered without the knowledge or consent of the Defendant [set out particulars].
4.
Accordingly, the Bond is no longer binding upon the Defendant.
