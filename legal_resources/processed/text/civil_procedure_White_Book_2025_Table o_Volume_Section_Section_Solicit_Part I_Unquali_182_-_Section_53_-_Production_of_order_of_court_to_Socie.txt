Section 7C - The Solicitors Act 1974
Solicitors Act 1974
Section 53
Part I
Unqualified persons acting as solicitors
53. Production of order of court to Society
7C-16
Where an order is made by the High Court or the Court of Appeal that the name of a solicitor be struck off the roll, or
that a solicitor be suspended from practice, the proper officer of the court shall forthwith send a copy of the order to the
Society, and the Society shall enter a note of the order on the roll against the name of the solicitor and, where the order
so directs, shall strike that name off the roll.
