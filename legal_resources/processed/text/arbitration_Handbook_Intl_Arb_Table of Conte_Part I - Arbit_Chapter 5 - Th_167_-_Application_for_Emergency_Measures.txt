Application for Emergency Measures
29-20
The Application for Emergency Measures is dealt with in detail in art.1 of the Emergency Arbitrator Rules. That provision
mirrors art.4 of the Rules regarding the filing of a Request for Arbitration. As with the latter, the Application is to be filed with
the Secretariat of the ICC Court. Article 1(1) of the Emergency Arbitrator Rules clarifies that the Application can be submitted
to any of the offices of the ICC Court’s Secretariat specified in the Internal Rules of the Court in App.II of the Rules. The 2021
ICC Note provides as follows with respect to filing an Application for Emergency Measures:
 
“73.Parties who wish to file an Application for Emergency Measures (‘Application’) should inform the
Secretariat as soon as possible and preferably before submitting the Application. If the Application precedes
the Request for Arbitration, parties should send an email to: emergencyarbitrator@iccwbo.org. If the
Application is related to an ongoing arbitration, parties should contact the ICC case management team to
which the arbitration has been assigned.”
