Emergency Measures
29-19
Article 29(1) defines Emergency Measures as “urgent interim or conservatory measures that cannot await the constitution of
an arbitral tribunal”. The reference to interim or conservatory measures is a reference to the measures as discussed in art.28(1).
The requirements for such measures is discussed under art.28(1). Those requirements are equally applicable under art.29(1).
However, in addition, the applicant must satisfy the Emergency Arbitrator that the Emergency Measures cannot await the
constitution of the Tribunal. Therefore, there must be a specific urgency linked to the period prior to transmittal of the file to
the Tribunal.
