Rule 45_35
45.35 Where the court orders the claim is not suitable to be determined under the Stage 3 Procedure and the
claimant is a child 1
7B-50+
Where—
(a) the claimant is a child; and
(b) at a settlement hearing or the Stage 3 hearing the court orders that the claim is not suitable to be determined under
the Stage 3 Procedure,
the court will order the defendant to pay—
(i) the Stage 1 and 2 fixed costs; and
(ii) the Stage 3 Type A, B and C fixed costs.
