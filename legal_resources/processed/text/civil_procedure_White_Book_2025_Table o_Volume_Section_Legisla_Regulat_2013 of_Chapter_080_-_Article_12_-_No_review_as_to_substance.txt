Regulation (EU) No.606/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 12 June 2013 on mutual recognition of
protection measures in civil matters
Chapter II Recognition and Enforcement of Protection Measures
Article 12 No review as to substance
5-165+
Under no circumstances may a protection measure be reviewed as to its substance by the competent court.
