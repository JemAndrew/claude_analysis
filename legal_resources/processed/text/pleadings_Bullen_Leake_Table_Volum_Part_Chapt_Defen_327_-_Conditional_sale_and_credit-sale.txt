Conditional sale and credit-sale
25-50
A high proportion of conditional sale and credit-sale agreements will be regulated by the Consumer Credit Act 1974 and this
number will increase when the Consumer Credit Act 2006 removes all financial limits for regulation on 6 April 2008. These
regulated agreements are subject to highly specialised and technical rules and the reader is referred to Section 20: Credit, Finance
and Leasing.
