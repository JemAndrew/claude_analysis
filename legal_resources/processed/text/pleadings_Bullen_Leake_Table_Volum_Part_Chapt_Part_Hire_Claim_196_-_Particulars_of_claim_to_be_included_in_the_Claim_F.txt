Hire and chattel leasing agreements
Claim by owner for delivery of the goods based on arrears of rent and termination by notice (regulated agreement)
(Consumer Credit Act Procedure)
Particulars of claim to be included in the Claim Form N1 or served with it
1.
20-F11
By a hire agreement in writing dated [date], the Claimant hired to the Defendant [identify the goods] (“the goods”). A copy
of the agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
The agreement was regulated by the Consumer Credit Act 1974 (“the CCA”) and this is a Consumer Credit Act claim
subject to the Consumer Credit Act procedure.
3.
By the agreement the Claimant hired the goods to the Defendant for a term of x months at a monthly rent of £[..........]
payable on the first day of each month.
4.
Clause 3 of the agreement provided that the Defendant should pay interest of x% over PQ Bank Plc base rate on all overdue
sums.
5.
Clause 10 of the agreement provided that, if the Defendant should fail to pay the rent when it fell due, the Claimant should
be entitled by notice to determine the agreement and the hiring.
6.
In breach of the agreement the Defendant failed to pay the rent due on [date] or any rent thereafter. On [date] the rent
was £[..........] in arrear.

7.
By a default and termination notice dated [date] (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim) the Claimant required the
Defendant to remedy the breach within fourteen days and gave notice that, in default, upon the expiry of the notice the
agreement and the hiring would terminate.
8.
The Defendant failed to remedy the breach within fourteen days or at all and on [date] the agreement and the hiring
terminated. On the said date the Defendant became liable to deliver up the goods to the Claimant.
9.
The Defendant has failed to pay the Claimant the arrears of rent or to deliver up the goods to the Claimant.
10.
At the date of issue of these proceedings the value of the goods is £[..........].
The Claimant therefore claims:
 
(1)Delivery up of the goods or payment of £[..........] its value.
 
(2)£[..........] arrears of rent.
 
(3)£[..........] contractual interest alternatively interest pursuant to s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984 accrued from
[date when each sum became due] to the date of the issue of the claim and continuing at the daily rate of £[..........]
until judgment.
