Claim by vendor for non-acceptance of shares
1.
16-E4
By a contract in writing dated [date] it was agreed between the claimant and the defendant that the claimant would sell to
the defendant [number] [class] shares of [nominal amount] each in E.F. Plc at the price of [amount] per share.
2.
By clause [number] of the contract it was agreed that the sale should be completed on [date].
3.
By a letter dated [date] the defendant wrongfully refused to complete the contract and accept the said shares.
4.
By reason of the defendant’s breach of contract the claimant has suffered loss and damage.
Particulars
 
(i)Difference between the market price of the said shares at the contractual completion date and the contract price;
 
(ii)Calls amounting to £[amount] paid by the claimant since the contractual completion date.
AND the claimant claims damages, interest thereon pursuant to statute and costs.
