Regulation (EU) No.606/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 12 June 2013 on mutual recognition of
protection measures in civil matters
Chapter III General and Final Provisions
Article 15 Legalisation and other similar formalities
5-168+
No legalisation or other similar formality shall be required for documents issued in a participating Member State
in the context of this Regulation.
