“Opting In” to the Expedited Procedure Rules
30-21
When parties wish to adopt the Expedited Procedure Provisions regardless of the amount in dispute or based on a different
threshold amount, they may also do so, either in the arbitration clause, or at the time of starting the arbitral proceedings. By the
end of 2020, 291 requests had been made to opt into the Expedited Procedure, of which any and 83 few have been accepted. 655
