20-F35
The first Defendant is a [specific nature of business] and the second Defendant is a finance company.
2.
In or about [date], the Claimant was minded to purchase [identify the goods] (“the goods”) which was being offered for
sale by the first Defendant. The goods were described [state manner and nature of the description] and the first Defendant
orally represented to the Claimant that [set out representations relied on].
3.
Acting in reliance on those representations, on [date] the Claimant orally agreed to purchase the goods from the first
Defendant for the sum of £[..........] (“the sale agreement”).
4.
It was an express term of the sale agreement that [identify all express terms relied on].
5.
By a written agreement dated [date], (“the loan agreement”) the second Defendant agreed to advance the sum of £[..........]
to the Claimant for the purpose of paying purchasing the goods. A copy of the loan agreement is annexed to this Claim
Form. It was a necessarily implied term of the loan agreement that, if the Claimant rescinded the sale agreement, the loan
agreement would also be rescinded.
6.
The loan agreement was a borrower-lender-supplier agreement within s.75 of the Consumer Credit Act 1974 (“the CCA”).
7.
In pursuance of the sale agreement, the Claimant paid the purchase price of £[..........]:

(a)as to £[..........] by a cheque; and
 
(b)as to £[..........] by means of a payment of the moneys advanced by the second Defendant.
8.
The said representations were false and the first Defendant was in breach of the sale agreement in that [give details of
misrepresentation and breach of contract].
9.
On [date], the Claimant discovered the matters set out in paragraph 8 [set out the circumstances of the discovery] and
became entitled to rescind the sale agreement.
10.
By a letter dated [date] to the first Defendant (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim) the Claimant duly rescinded the
sale agreement and on [date] returned the car to the first Defendant.
11.
By reason of the said rescission, the first Defendant became liable to repay the purchase price to the Claimant. Upon receipt
of such payment, the Claimant will account to the second Defendant for the proportion of the price represented by the loan.
The first Defendant has failed or refused to repay the price or any part thereof.
12.
Pursuant to s.75(1) of the CCA and to its implied term, the Claimant was entitled to rescind the loan agreement and by a
letter dated [date] to the second Defendant (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim) the Claimant duly rescinded the
loan agreement and is entitled to repayment of all moneys paid thereunder, namely £[..........].
The Claimant therefore claims against the first Defendant:
 
(1)A declaration that he has validly rescinded the sale agreement alternatively rescission thereof.
 
(2)Repayment of the purchase price of £[..........].
 
(3)Interest at the rate of x% pursuant to s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984 from [date] to [date] £[..........] and
continuing at £[..........] per day to the date of judgment herein.
The Claimant therefore claims against the second Defendant:
 
(1)A declaration that he has validly rescinded the loan agreement alternatively rescission thereof.
 
(2)Repayment of the all moneys paid under the loan agreement: £[..........].
 
(3)Interestat the rate of x% pursuant to s.69 of the County Courts Act 1984 from [date] to [date] £[..........] and
continuing at £[..........] per day to judgment.
