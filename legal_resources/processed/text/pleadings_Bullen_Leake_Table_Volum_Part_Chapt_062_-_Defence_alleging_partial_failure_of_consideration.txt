Defence alleging partial failure of consideration
1.
10-E20
It is admitted that the Defendant drew and delivered to the Claimant the cheque for £2,000 sued upon by the Claimant. It
is admitted that the Defendant countermanded payment of the cheque.
2.
The Defendant drew the cheque in consideration of the payment of the price agreed between the parties for the delivery
by the Claimant to the Defendant of four television sets at a price of £500 each.
3.
The Claimant failed to deliver all four sets, and has delivered only one which the Defendant accepted. In the premises the
consideration for the cheque has failed save to the extent of £500.
4.
In those circumstances it is admitted that the Defendant is liable upon the cheque to the extent of £500, but denied that he
is liable for the sum claimed or any other sum as alleged or at all.
