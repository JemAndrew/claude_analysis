When the “Consumer Credit Act Procedure” (“CCA Procedure”) applies
Category 5: Claims by debtors or hirers for time orders under s.129(1)(b) or (1)(ba)
20-47
These must be brought in the county court.
The claim form must contain the information prescribed by CPR PD 7B.7.3. The CCA Procedure applies so that the particulars
of claim must be served with the claim form which must include or be accompanied by a notice of the hearing date. If the claim
is made under s.129(1)(ba) (i.e. after the appropriate notice has been served on the creditor under s.129A(1)(a)), the claimant
must attach to the particulars of claim a copy of the notice—CPR PD 7B7.3A.
