Mandatory application
Article 30(2)
“The Expedited Procedure Rules set forth in Appendix VI shall apply if:
 
a) the amount in dispute does not exceed the limit set out in Article 1(2) of Appendix VI at the time of the
communication referred to in Article 1(3) of that Appendix; or
 
b) the parties so agree.”
30-24
Article 30(2) provides for the application of the Expedited Procedure Rules, i.e. of App.VI, in two situations: The amount in
dispute is below the threshold amount, or the parties have agreed to their application irrespective of the threshold.
