The Defendant denies that the Claimant is entitled to the return of the sailing vessel referred to in paragraph 1 of the
Particulars of Claim.
2.
By an oral agreement made in or about [date], the Defendant agreed to carry out works of repair and improvement to the
[goods claimed by the Claimant], including [summarise agreed works].
3.
The Defendant carried out the work for which he was entitled to be paid a reasonable amount, namely, £[..........]. By a
letter dated [date], a copy of which is annexed to this Defence, the Defendant requested payment of the said sum from
the Claimant.
4.
The Claimant has failed or refused to pay the said sum or any part thereof.
5.
In the premises, the Defendant is entitled to exercise a lien over the vessel until the Claimant pays the amount due.
