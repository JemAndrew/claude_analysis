Claim by drawer pleading antecedent debt, and circumstances dispensing with presentment
1.
10-E18
In or about March 2014 the Claimant advanced the sum of £2,000 to the Defendant by way of loan which was agreed
orally to be repayable upon demand.
2.
On 4 March 2015, the Claimant having demanded repayment of the loan, the Defendant drew and delivered to the Claimant
a cheque in the sum of £2,000 drawn upon his bankers ABC Bank Plc, a copy of which is attached marked “A”.
3.
On 5 March 2015 the Defendant instructed his bankers ABC Bank Plc not to honour the cheque and advised the Claimant
orally that he had done so. In the circumstances presentment for payment was dispensed with and/or waived.
4.
The cheque remains unpaid, and the Defendant has failed to repay the sum lent to him by the Claimant.
5.
In the premises the Claimant is entitled to and claims. [Continue as above or as appropriate.]
