20-F31
In or about [date], the Claimant was minded to acquire [identify the goods] (“the goods”) offered for sale by XY Limited
(“the supplier”).
2.
In the course of negotiations the supplier orally represented to the Claimant that [summarise the representations relied on].
3.
Acting in reliance on the said representation(s), the Claimant agreed to a transaction whereby the supplier sold the goods
to the Defendant and the Claimant entered into a hire-purchase with the Defendant to take the goods on hire-purchase.
4.
By a written agreement dated [date], the Defendant let the goods on hire-purchase to the Claimant. A copy of the agreement
is annexed to this Claim Form.
5.
The cash price of the goods was £[..........], the deposit was £[..........], the total charge for credit was £[..........] and the hire-
purchase balance of £[..........] was payable by x equal consecutive monthly instalments of £[..........]. The total purchase
price was £[..........].
6.
The said agreement was a regulated borrower-lender-supplier agreement within the meaning of the Consumer Credit Act
1974 (“the CCA”). In the premises the representation set out in paragraph 2 was made on behalf of the Defendant pursuant
to s.56(2) of the CCA.
7.
By the Supply of Goods (Implied Terms) Act 1973, s.10, the following were terms of the hire purchase agreement:
