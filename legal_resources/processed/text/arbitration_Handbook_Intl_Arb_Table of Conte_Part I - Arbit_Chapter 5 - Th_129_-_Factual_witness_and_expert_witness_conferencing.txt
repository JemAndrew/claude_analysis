Factual witness and expert witness conferencing

In some instances, the factual and expert issues are so intertwined that the Tribunal may wish to hear a panel of factual witnesses
and experts. This type of procedure basically relegates the lawyers to the role of observers and can be very difficult to manage.
In fact, the joint panel approach may be most appropriate where the factual witnesses are engineers who have worked on the
litigious project and have technical expertise that is on the level of that of an expert.
