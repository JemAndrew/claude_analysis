1.
22-F3
By a factoring agreement made on the [date] between the Claimant and the Defendant, the Defendant assigned to the
Claimant all debts then and at any time thereafter owing in respect of its sales of motor cars to customers (“the customers”).
A copy of the factoring agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
It was a term of the factoring agreement that, in the event of a customer raising a claim against the Claimant or the Defendant
arising out of the performance of the sales contract, the Defendant would indemnify the Claimant in respect of all sums
that it might become liable to pay the customer, including costs, and all sums that the Claimant was unable to recover from
the customer by reason of the performance of the contract of sale.
3.
On [date], Tom Smith (“the debtor”) purchased [identify the vehicle] (“the car”) from the Defendant at a price of £[..........].
The purchase price of the car thereupon became a debt (“the debt”) owing to the Defendant and payable within 30 days.
By reason of the factoring agreement, it was assigned to the Claimant.
4.
The Claimant acting for and on behalf of the Defendant served the debtor with notice of assignment of the debt on [date].
5.
The debtor failed or neglected to pay the balance of £[..........] of the debt and thereafter claimed that he was entitled to set-
off against the debt damages by reason of breaches of contract committed by the Defendant. Full particulars of the debtor’s
claim are set out in the Particulars of Claim brought by him in the [..........] County Court in action number [number], a
copy of which has already been served upon the Defendant.
6.
The Debtor’s claim was settled by the Claimant for £[..........] plus costs of £[..........]. The Claimant incurred costs of
£[..........]. In addition, the Claimant was unable to recover the balance of the debt of £[..........]. The Claimant has thereby
suffered loss and damage of £[..........].
