Civil Jurisdiction and Judgments Act 1982
Section 3c
Part I Implementation of the Conventions
Interpretation
3D.— The 2005 Hague Convention to have the force of law
5-11. 5
(1) The 2005 Hague Convention shall have the force of law in the United Kingdom.
(2) For the purposes of this Act the 2005 Hague Convention is to be read together with the declarations made by the
United Kingdom on 28th September 2020 under Article 21 of the Convention, concerning insurance contracts
(3) For convenience of reference there are set out in Schedules 3F and 3FA respectively—
(a) the English text of the 2005 Hague Convention;
(b) the declarations referred to in subsection (2).
Note
5-11. 5. 1Section 3D inserted by the Private International Law (Implementation of Agreements) Act 2020 (c.24) s.1(2), with effect from 31
December 2020 (IP completion day). Subsection (2) amended and subs.(3) substituted by the Civil Jurisdiction and Judgments
(2005 Hague Convention and 2007 Hague Convention) (Amendment) Regulations 2022 (SI 2022/77) reg.2(2), with effect from
26 January 2022.
