Parties may seek different types of financial measures under art.28. Among the most frequent applications are those for security
for costs. As discussed below, although granting of security for costs is still exceptional, orders for security for costs appear to
be more frequent now than previously. In addition, as noted in a recent survey of users of international arbitration, the lack of
effective sanctions during the arbitral process is viewed as a major weakness in international arbitration. Therefore, there is an
attempt to find solutions to problems during the arbitration by recourse to provisions such as art.28. 489
