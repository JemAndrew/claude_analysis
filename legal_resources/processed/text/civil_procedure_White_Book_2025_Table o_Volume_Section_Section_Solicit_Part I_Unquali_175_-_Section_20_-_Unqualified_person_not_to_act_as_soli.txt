Unqualified persons acting as solicitors
20.— Unqualified person not to act as solicitor
7C-2
(1) No unqualified person is to act as a solicitor.
(2) Any person who contravenes subsection (1) is guilty of an offence and liable on conviction on indictment to
imprisonment for not more than 2 years or to a fine, or to both.
Note
7C-3
Substituted by the Legal Services Act 2007 Sch.16 para.25, with effect from 1 January 2010 (SI 2009/3250)).
