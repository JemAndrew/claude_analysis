Solicitors Act 1974
Section 25
Part I
Unqualified persons acting as solicitors
25.— Costs where unqualified person acts as solicitor
7C-8
(1) No costs in respect of anything done by any unqualified person acting as a solicitor shall be recoverable by him,
or by any other person, in any action, suit or matter.
(2) Nothing in subsection (1) shall prevent the recovery of money paid or to be paid by a solicitor on behalf of a client
in respect of anything done by the solicitor while acting for the client without holding a practising certificate in force
if that money would have been recoverable if he had held such a certificate when so acting.
Note
7C-9
Amended by the Courts and Legal Services Act 1990 Sch.18 para.12.
