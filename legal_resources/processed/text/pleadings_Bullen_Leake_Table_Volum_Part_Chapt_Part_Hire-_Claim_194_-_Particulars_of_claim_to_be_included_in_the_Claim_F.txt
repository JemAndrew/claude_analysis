Particulars of claim to be included in the Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
20-F9
By a conditional sale agreement in writing dated [date] the Claimant sold to the Defendant on conditional sale [identify
the goods] (“the goods”). A copy of the agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
The agreement was regulated by the Consumer Credit Act 1974 (“the CCA”) and this is a Consumer Credit Act claim
subject to the Consumer Credit Act procedure.
3.
The cash price of the goods was £[..........], no deposit was payable and the price together with charges of £[..........] was
payable by x equal consecutive monthly instalments of £[..........]. The total purchase price was £[..........].
4.
By clause 3 of the agreement, the Defendant agreed to pay interest of x% over PQ Bank Plc base rate on all overdue sums.
5.
Clause 10 of the agreement provided that if the Defendant should fail to pay any instalment when it fell due or commit any
other breach of the agreement and should thereafter fail to comply with a default notice, the Claimant should be entitled
to call by notice for an acceleration in payment calculated in accordance with the terms of that clause. Upon the expiry
of the acceleration notice, the Defendant would become liable to pay the sum set out in the notice and title in the goods
would vest in the Defendant.
6.
The following are the particulars required by the Civil Procedure Rules, PD16.6.2:
 
(1)the date of the agreement: [date]
