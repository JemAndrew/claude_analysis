Claims by the buyer
25-19
Claims by the buyer fall into two main categories: claims based on the failure of the seller to make proper delivery of the goods;
and claims based on the delivery of goods which do not conform to the terms of the contract. Claims in the latter category may,
in turn, depend on whether the term broken is a condition or a warranty.
