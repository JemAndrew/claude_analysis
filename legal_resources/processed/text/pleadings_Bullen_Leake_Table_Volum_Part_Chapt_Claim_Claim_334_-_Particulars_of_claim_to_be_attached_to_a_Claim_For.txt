Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
25-F4
The Claimant and the Defendant both carry on business as dealers in motor vehicles.
2.
By an agreement in writing dated [date] the Claimant agreed to supply to the Defendant (e.g. a van) [identify the goods]
(“the van”) on approval on terms that, if the Defendant approved the van within thirty days of delivery, he should pay to
the Claimant the price of £[..........]. A copy of the agreement is annexed to these Particulars of Claim.
3.
By clause [..........] of the agreement it was provided that the Defendant should be deemed to have approved the van if
(among other things) he resold it or let it on hire.
4.
On [date] the Claimant delivered the van to the Defendant.
5.
On [date] the Claimant sold (hired) the van to PQ and hence approved the van. Property in the van thereupon passed to
the Defendant who became liable to pay the sum of £[..........] to the Claimant.
6.
The Defendant has failed to pay the Claimant the sum of £[..........] or any part thereof.
7.
By an agreement in writing dated [date] the Claimant agreed to supply to the Defendant (e.g. a car) [identify the goods]
(“the car”) on sale or return on terms that, if the Defendant did not return the car to the Claimant within 14 days of delivery,
he should pay to the Claimant the price of £[..........]. A copy of the agreement is annexed to these Particulars of Claim.
