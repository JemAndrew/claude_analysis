Section 7 - Legal Representatives—Costs and Litigation Funding
Section 7C - The Solicitors Act 1974
Solicitors’ (Non Contentious Business) Remuneration Order 1994
12.— Miscellaneous provisions
7C-205+
(1) After an application has been made by a solicitor for a remuneration certificate the client may pay the bill in full
without invalidating the application.
(2) A solicitor and entitled person may agree in writing to waive the provisions of sub-paragraphs (a) or (b) of articles
9 or 10.
(3) A solicitor may take from his client security for the payment of any costs, including the amount of any interest
to which the solicitor may become entitled under article 14.
Note
7C-205.
1+
Whole SI revoked except in its application to non-contentious business for which bills are delivered before 11 August 2009, by
