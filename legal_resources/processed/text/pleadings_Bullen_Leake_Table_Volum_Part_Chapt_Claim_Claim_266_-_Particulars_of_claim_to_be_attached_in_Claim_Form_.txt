Particulars of claim to be attached in Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
21-F5
By a written agreement (“the agency agreement”) made on the [date], the Claimant agreed to act for the Defendant as its
agent in negotiating and agreeing contracts for the purchase of [identify the goods]. A copy of the agency agreement is
annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
In a written agreement (“the sales agreement”) made on the [date] and signed by the Claimant in his own name and as
principal, the Claimant agreed to purchase from Y Limited [identify goods purchased] at a price of £[..........].
3.
In entering into the sales agreement, the Claimant did so pursuant to the agency agreement and on behalf of the Defendant.
4.
By a letter dated [date] from the Claimant to the Defendant, the Claimant sought payment from the Defendant of the price
due under the sales agreement but the Defendant has failed to pay the same. A copy of the letter is annexed to this Claim
Form.
5.
It was an implied term of the agency agreement that the Defendant would pay the Claimant such sums as the Claimant was
liable to pay suppliers under contracts entered into by him on behalf of the Defendant pursuant to the agency agreement
and/or to indemnify the Claimant.
6.
Y Limited has threatened to sue the Claimant for the price payable under the sales agreement.
7.
The Claimant is entitled to the sum of £[..........] which he is liable to pay under the sales agreement and/or to an indemnity
from the Defendant in respect of that amount and all other sums that the Claimant is or may become liable to pay Y
