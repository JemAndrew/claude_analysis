Claim upon a bill of exchange by the payee against the drawer

The Defendant drew upon AB a Bill of Exchange (“the Bill”) for £10,000 together with interest at a rate of 2 per cent per
month, dated 31 January 2015, and payable to the Claimant on 30 June 2000. A copy of the Bill is annexed marked (1).
2.
On 30 June 2015 the Claimant duly presented the Bill to AB for payment.
3.
Despite the due presentment of the Bill, it was dishonoured by nonpayment.
4.
Due Notice of Dishonour was given in writing to the Defendant on Se25 ptember 2015 and a copy is annexed marked (2).
[And/or] Notice of Dishonour was dispensed with because [the Defendant countermanded payment of the Bill] [or..........in
accordance with s.50 of the Bills of Exchange Act 1882].
[And/or] Notice of Dishonour was waived by [..........].
5.
The Claimant as payee claims payment as follows:
 
(1)The amount of the Bill being £10,000 together with,
 
(2)interest as provided in it at a rate of 2 per cent per month for [..........] months amounting to £[..........] at the date
hereof.
6.
The Claimant claims continuing interest pursuant to s.57(1) of the Bills of Exchange Act 1882 at the rate of 2 per cent per
month; or alternatively pursuant to s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981 at the same rate, equivalent to a rate of £[..........]
per day.
