Claims by the seller
Other remedies
25-18
The unpaid seller has available various other remedies such as a lien over the goods and a right of stoppage in transit. These
rights will almost always be exercised by way of a defence to a claim by the buyer and will be examined in more detail in the
Defences section at para.25-F37.
