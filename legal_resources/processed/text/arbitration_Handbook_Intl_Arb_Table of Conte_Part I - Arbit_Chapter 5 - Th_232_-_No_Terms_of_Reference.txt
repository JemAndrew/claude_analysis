Article 3(1) of App.VI excludes Terms of Reference, one of the hallmarks of ICC arbitration, from the ambit of expedited
procedures. ICC arbitrators acting under the Expedited Procedure Provisions remain obliged to prepare a procedural timetable
during the case management conference under art.24. They will also continue to issue a first procedural order to set out the
procedural rules of the proceedings in consultation, and if possible, agreement with the parties. Furthermore, if the arbitration
takes place in a jurisdiction which requires the preparation of Terms of Reference or a similar type of document such a document
should be signed by the parties and the sole arbitrator to assure the enforceability of the award. 667
