Rule 45_8
45.8 Application of fixed recoverable costs 1
7B-23+
Subject to rule 45.12, the only costs which are to be allowed are—
(a) fixed recoverable costs calculated in accordance with rule 45.9;
(b) disbursements allowed in accordance with rule 45.10; and
(c) a success fee allowed in accordance with rule 45.11.
(Rule 45.12 provides for where a party issues a claim for more than the fixed recoverable costs.)
Effect of rule
7B-23.
1+
This rule applies in relation to a “pre-commencement funding arrangement”; see para.7B-19+, and paras 48.0.1 to 48.0.4
(“Continuing application of CPR Pts 43 to 48 and Costs Practice Direction as in force immediately before 1 April 2013”). See
further commentary following r.45.10 (Disbursements) and r.45.11 (Success fee) below.
