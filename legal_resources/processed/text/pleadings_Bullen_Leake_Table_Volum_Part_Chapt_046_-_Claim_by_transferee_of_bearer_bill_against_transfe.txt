Claim by transferee of bearer bill against transferor
1.
10-E4
On 30 March 2015 the Defendant purported to negotiate by transferring to the Claimant a bill of exchange purporting to be
drawn by AB Ltd upon CD Ltd and payable to bearer, purporting to have been accepted by CD Ltd, in the sum of £10,000
and payable on demand (“the Bill”).
2.
The Claimant accepted the Bill in payment of the price of a motor car then sold by him and delivered to the Defendant.
3.
In fact the signatures of both AB Ltd and CD Ltd upon the bill were forgeries.
4.
In the circumstances the Claimant claims the sum of £10,000 together with interest thereon pursuant to s.35A of the Senior
Courts Act 1981 at such rate and for such time as the Court sees fit.
