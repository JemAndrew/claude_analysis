Section 5 - International Jurisdiction and Enforcement of Judgments
Part - Legislation
Civil Jurisdiction and Judgments Act 1982 (Gibraltar) Order 1997
2.
5-102
(a) Provision corresponding to that made by the provisions of the 1968 Convention specified in paragraph (b) as
they had effect immediately before IP completion day shall apply, so far as relevant, for the purpose of regulating,
as between the United Kingdom and Gibraltar, the jurisdiction of courts and the recognition and enforcement of
judgments.
(b) Those provisions are—
(i) Titles I–V;
(ii) Articles 54 and 57; and
(iii) Article 65 and the Protocol referred to therein.
Note
5-102. 1 Article 2(a) amended by the Civil Jurisdiction and Judgments (Amendment) (EU Exit) Regulations 2019 (SI 2019/479)
reg.73(2), with effect from 31 December 2020 and the Civil, Criminal and Family Justice (Amendment) (EU Exit) Regulations
2020 (SI 2020/1493) reg.5(2)(e)(i), with effect from 31 December 2020.
