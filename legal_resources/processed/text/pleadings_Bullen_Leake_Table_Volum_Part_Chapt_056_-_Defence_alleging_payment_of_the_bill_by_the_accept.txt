Defence alleging payment of the bill by the acceptor and consequent discharge
1.
10-E14
It is admitted that the Defendant drew the bill sued upon.
2.
On [date] and before these proceedings AB Ltd, who accepted the bill, paid all sums due upon it to the Claimant, and the
bill was thereby discharged.
3.
In the circumstances it is denied that the Defendant is liable upon the bill as alleged or at all.
