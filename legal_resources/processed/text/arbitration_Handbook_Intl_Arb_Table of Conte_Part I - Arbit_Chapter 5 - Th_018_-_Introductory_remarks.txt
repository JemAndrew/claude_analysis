Introductory remarks
18-1
Article 18 deals in three short paragraphs with the place of arbitration, sometimes also known as the seat of the arbitration. 24
The basic principle implicit in art.18 and explicit in many laws on arbitration, including in art.20 of the UNCITRAL Model
Law, is that the parties may choose the place or seat of arbitration. However, for an ICC arbitration, there must be a place of
arbitration. 25
