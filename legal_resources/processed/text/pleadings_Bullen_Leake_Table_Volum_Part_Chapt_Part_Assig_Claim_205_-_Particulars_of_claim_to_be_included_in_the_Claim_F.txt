Particulars of claim to be included in the Claim Form N1 or served with it
1.
20-F20
By a written agreement made [date], (“the loan agreement”) XY Limited advanced to the Defendant the sum of £[..........].
A copy of the loan agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
The said loan with interest of £[..........] was payable by equal consecutive monthly instalments of £[..........].
3.
By clause 6, the loan agreement provided that, if the Defendant failed to pay any instalment on the due date, the lender
should be entitled to serve a notice terminating the loan whereupon the balance of the loan and interest should immediately
become payable by the Defendant.
4.
By an assignment in writing dated [date] (“the assignment”) XY Limited assigned the agreement to the Claimant. A copy
of the assignment is annexed to this Claim Form.
5.
By a notice in writing dated [date] (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim), the Claimant duly notified the Defendant
of the assignment.
6.
The Defendant failed to pay the instalments due from [date] to [date] in the total sum of £[..........]. On [date] the instalments
were £[..........] in arrear.
7.
By a notice dated [date] (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim), the Claimant as it was entitled to do duly terminated
the loan. In consequence the balance of the loan and interest became payable by the Defendant in the sum of £[..........].
