Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
27-F3
By an agreement in writing (“the overdraft agreement”) made [date] between the Defendant and X Bank Plc (“the Bank”),
the Bank agreed to grant the Defendant overdraft facilities in respect of his professional account. A copy of the overdraft
agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
By the terms of the overdraft agreement, all sums due from the Defendant to the Bank were repayable on demand.
3.
By a guarantee in writing (“the guarantee”) made [date] between the Claimant and the Bank, the Claimant agreed that in
consideration of the Bank according overdraft facilities to the Defendant, the Claimant would guarantee all moneys which
should be due and owing from the Defendant to the Bank and should pay the same to the Bank on demand. A copy of
the guarantee is annexed to this Claim Form.
4.
On [date] the Bank duly demanded of the Defendant repayment of the sum of £[..........] within seven days. The Defendant
failed to pay the said sum to the Bank or any part thereof.
5.
By a letter to the Claimant dated [date] (copy annexed to these Particulars of Claim) the bank duly demanded payment of
the said sum of £[..........] pursuant to the guarantee.
6.
On [date] the Claimant paid the said sum of £25,000 to the Bank.
7.
In the premises, the Claimant is entitled to recover the said sum of £[..........] from the Defendant.
