Claim by broker for money paid and commission and brokerage
1.
17-E1
The Claimant is a member of the Stock Exchange.
2.
By a client agreement in writing dated [date] the defendants engaged the claimant to provide investment management and
dealing services. A copy of the agreement is annexed.
3.
By clause 14.1 of the said client agreement the defendants agreed to pay interest at a rate of 4 per cent over the base rate
for the time being of [name] Bank on sums overdue for payment to the claimant.
4.
As at [date] the defendants were indebted to the claimant in respect of dealings in investments in the net sum of
£[amount]. Full particulars of the account between the claimant and the defendants have been supplied to the defendants.
Notwithstanding demands the defendants have failed to pay the said sums or any part thereof to the claimant.
5.
Interest on the sums due to the claimant amounts to [amount], down to [date]. Interest continues to accrue thereon at the
rate of [amount], per day. Particulars of the interest calculation are given in the Schedule hereto.
And the Claimant claims:
 
(1)The sum of, £[amount];
 
(2)Interest thereon amounting to, £[amount] per day until judgment or sooner payment.
