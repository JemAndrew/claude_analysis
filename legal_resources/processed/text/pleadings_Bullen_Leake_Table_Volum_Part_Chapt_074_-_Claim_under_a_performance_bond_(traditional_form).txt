Claim under a performance bond (traditional form)
1.
11-E3
On [date], the Defendant executed a bond (“the Bond”) in the sum of £500,000 conditioned for the due performance by XY
Construction Ltd (“XY”) of a written contract made between the Claimant and XY dated [date] for the execution of certain
building works and their completion by [completion date] (“the Contract”). The Bond is attached to these Particulars of
Claim marked “A”.
2.
XY did not duly perform the Contract or complete the building works by [completion date] or at all. [Set out particulars
of the breach.]
3.
By reason of the matters set out above, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage [set out particulars].
4.
Further the Claimant is entitled to and claims interest pursuant to s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981 on such sum at such
rate and for such period as the Court shall determine.
AND the Claimant claims:
 
(1)Damages;
 
(2)Interest pursuant to s.35A of the Senior Courts Act 1981 to be assessed.
