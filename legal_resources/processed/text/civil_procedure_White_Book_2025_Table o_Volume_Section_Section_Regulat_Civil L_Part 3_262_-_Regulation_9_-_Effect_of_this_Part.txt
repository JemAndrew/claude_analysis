Section 7 - Legal Representatives—Costs and Litigation Funding
Section 7D - Legal Aid
Regulations under the Legal Aid Sentencing and Punishment of Offenders Act 2012
Civil Legal Aid (Costs) Regulations 2013
Part 3 Costs Orders Against a Legally Aided Party and the Lord Chancellor
9.— Effect of this Part
7D-112+
(1) This Part applies where cost protection applies.
(2) The court may not, except in accordance with this Part, make an order requiring the Lord Chancellor to pay the
whole or part of any costs incurred by a non-legally aided party because the proceedings are relevant proceedings
as regards another party.
