VI. - Pre-Action Protocol for Low Value Personal Injury Claims in Road Traffic Accidents
 
Related Practice Directions
45.39 Adjournment 1
7B-54+
Where the court adjourns a settlement hearing or a Stage 3 hearing it may, in its discretion, order a party to pay—
(a) an additional amount of the Stage 3 Type B fixed costs; and
(b) any court fee for that adjournment.
