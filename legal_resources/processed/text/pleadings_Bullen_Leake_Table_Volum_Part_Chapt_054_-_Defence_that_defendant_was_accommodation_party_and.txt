Defence that defendant was accommodation party and that claimant gave no value for the bill
1.
10-E12
It is admitted that the AB Ltd drew and the Defendant accepted the bill sued upon as alleged by the Claimant.
2.
The Defendant signed the bill at the request of the AB Ltd and for the accommodation of AB Ltd. There was no
consideration for the Defendant’s acceptance.
3.
It is admitted that AB Ltd negotiated the bill to the Claimant. However the Claimant gave no consideration for the bill
and is not a holder in due course or for value of it.
4.
In the circumstances it is denied that the Defendant is liable to the Claimant upon the bill as alleged or at all.
