Defence

24-F4
It is admitted that, subject to the set-off hereinafter set out, the Defendant is liable for the storage charges referred to in
the Particulars of Claim.
2.
As a bailee for reward, the Claimant had a duty of care in relation to the storage of the Defendant’s goods.
3.
At a date unknown to the Defendant, the Claimant parted with possession of the goods set out in the Schedule annexed
to the Defence. Alternatively, the Claimant negligently permitted the said items to be stolen by leaving the doors of the
warehouse unlocked.
4.
By reason of the foregoing, the Claimant is liable to the Defendant for the value of the goods lost or stolen, namely,
£[..........].
5.
The Defendant is entitled to set-off against the sum claimed the loss and damage occasioned by the Claimant’s breach
of duty.
