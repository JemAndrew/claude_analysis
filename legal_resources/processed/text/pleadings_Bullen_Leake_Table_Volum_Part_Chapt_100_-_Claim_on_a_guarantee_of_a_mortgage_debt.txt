By the terms of a mortgage deed in writing (“the mortgage”) dated [date] between the Claimant and AB, the Claimant
advanced the sum of £[..........] to AB. By clause [..........] of the mortgage, AB covenanted to repay to the Claimant this
sum together with interest at the contractual rate of [..........] per cent per annum on demand by the Claimant.
2.
By a guarantee in writing dated [date] (“the guarantee”) the Defendant guaranteed all liabilities of AB to the Claimant. A
copy of the mortgage and the guarantee 43  is attached to the Particulars of Claim and marked “A”.
3.
The Claimant made demand on AB for the sum of £[..........] by letter dated [date]. AB has failed to make payment to the
Claimant under the terms of the mortgage.
4.
By a letter dated [date] the Claimant made demand on the Defendant under the terms of the guarantee for the sum of
£[..........]. The Defendant has failed to make payment of the sum demanded, or any sum.
5.
The Claimant is entitled to and claims payment of the sum of £[..........] from the Defendant under the terms of the guarantee.
6.
Further, the Claimant is entitled to and claims contractual interest under clause [..........] of the guarantee on the sum of
£[..........] at the rate of [..........] per cent per annum from [the date of demand or from such other date as specified in the
clauses of the sale agreement or guarantee respectively], amounting to £[..........] at [date of claim 44 ] and continuing at
a daily rate of £[..........].
AND the Claimant claims:
 
(1)The sum of £[..........] under the guarantee;
 
(2)Interest at the contractual rate, under paragraph 6 above.
