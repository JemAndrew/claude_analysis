2.
By an agreement in writing dated [date] (“the distribution agreement”), the Claimant engaged XY Limited to act as
distribution agent for its [products]. A copy of the agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
3.
By the distribution agreement, XY Limited agreed (among other things):
 
(a)to account to the Claimant for the proceeds of all sales of the products after deducting its sales commission;
 
(b)[set out any relevant obligation for breach of which the Claimant claims relief].
4.
By an indemnity agreement in writing made between the Claimant and the Defendant and dated [date] (“the indemnity”),
the Defendant agreed to indemnify the Claimant in respect of all sums due from XY Limited under the distribution
agreement and all losses sustained by the Claimant arising in connection with the distribution agreement. A copy of the
indemnity is annexed to this Claim Form.
5.
By clause 2 of the indemnity, the Defendant should be liable as a principal debtor and his liability should not be dependent
on any demand being made on XY Limited.
6.
In breach of the distribution agreement, XY Limited failed to account to the Claimant for the proceeds of sales between
[date] and [date] in the total sum of £[..........].
