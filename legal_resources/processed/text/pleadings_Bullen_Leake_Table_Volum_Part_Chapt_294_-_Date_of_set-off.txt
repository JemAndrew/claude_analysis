The sum claimed to be set off must have accrued due before the action was commenced. 26  A cross-claim arising after the date
of the proceedings must be raised in a separate action. For limitation purposes a claim by way of set-off or counterclaim is
deemed to be a separate action and to have been commenced on the same day as the original action (Limitation Act 1980 s.35).
