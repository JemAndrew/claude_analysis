Action for breach of condition or warranty
Installation
25-31
The CRA contains a provision not mirrored in the SGA. If installation of the goods is part of the contract and they are installed
by the trader or under his responsibility, then, if the goods are installed incorrectly, under CRA s.15, they do not conform to
the contract of supply. This cannot be excluded—CRA s.31.
