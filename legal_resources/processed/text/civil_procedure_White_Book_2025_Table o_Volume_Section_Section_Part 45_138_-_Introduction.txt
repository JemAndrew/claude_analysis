Editorial introduction
7B-18+ As originally enacted in the Civil Procedure Rules 1998 (SI 1998/3132), and brought into force on 26 April 1999, Pt 45 was
confined to five rules (rr.45.1–45.5) relating to fixed commencement costs and fixed costs on entry of judgment in claims for
the recovery of money or goods, with tables incorporated therein (Tables 1 to 3).
The Part was divided into two Sections when rr.45.7–45.14 (Section II) were added by the Civil Procedure (Amendment) Rules
2003 (SI 2003/2113). Subsequently, as the range of circumstances in which costs are fixed by rules of court was increased,
Sections III to VIII were added by, respectively, SI 2004/1306, SI 2004/2072, SI 2005/2292, SI 2010/621, SI 2010/1953 and
SI 2011/88. The background to Sections II to IV was explained by Dyson L.J. in Lamont v Burton [2007] EWCA Civ 429,
[2007] 1 W.L.R. 2814, CA, at para.6.
In Sections II to VI particular rules provide for the recovery, in addition to fixed costs, of “additional liability” costs.
Post-April 2013 application—Sections II to VI
7B-19+ As is explained immediately below, for a period post-April 2013 that cannot be fixed definitely, judges and practitioners will
in certain circumstances have need to have recourse to the provisions of Sections II to VI of the pre-April 2013 Pt 45 and their
supplementing practice directions.
With effect from 1 April 2013, by r.16 of the Civil Procedure (Amendment) Rules 2013 (SI 2013/262), Pts 44–48 as they stood
before that date (the “pre-April 2013 Costs Parts”) were substituted by Pts 44–48 (the “post-April 2013 Costs Parts”) as set out
in the Schedule to that statutory instrument. By CPR Update 60, the Costs Practice Direction supplementing the pre-April 2013
Costs Parts was revoked and replaced by several new practice directions. However, by operation of transitional and savings
provisions, various provisions in the pre-April 2013 Costs Parts (including Pt 45) and related practice directions continue to
have effect for certain purposes.
In Pt 48 as substituted by the Civil Procedure (Amendment) Rules 2013 (SI 2013/262), and brought into effect on 1 April 2013,
r.48.1(1) states that the provisions “relating to funding arrangements” in Pt 45 (and in Pt 43 and Pts 46–48), and the attendant
provisions of the Costs Practice Direction, will apply in relation to “a pre-commencement funding arrangement” (as defined in
r.48.2) as they were in force immediately before 1 April 2013, with such modifications (if any) as may be made by a practice
direction on or after that date.
For detailed explanation, see paras 48.0.1 to 48.0.3 (“Continuing application of CPR Part 43 to 48 and Costs Practice Direction
as in force immediately before April 1, 2013”). See also s.30 of the SCCO Guide 2013.

Post-April 2013 application—Sections I, VII and VIII
7B-21+ Although for a period post-April 2013 that cannot be fixed definitely, judges and practitioners will in certain circumstances
have need to have recourse to the provisions in Sections II to VI of the pre-April 2013 Pt 45, that should not be necessary in
relation to Sections I, VII and VIII of that Part.
As is explained immediately below, these Sections are replicated in Sections I, IV and V of the post-April 2013 Pt 45.
The rules in Section I of the pre-April 2013 Pt 45 (rr.45.1 to 45.6) were re-enacted in the post-April 2013 Pt 45 as Section
I (rr.45.1 to 45.8) without substantive amendment. The supplementing directions in ss.24, 24A and 25 of the Costs Practice
Direction were re-issued in Practice Direction 45 (paras 1.1 to 1.3). For texts of these provisions, see para.7BPD-7+.
The rules in Section VII of the pre-April 2013 Pt 45 (rr.45.41 to 45.43) were reenacted in the post-April 2013 Pt 45 as Section
IV (rr.45.30 to 45.32) without substantive amendment, but shortly afterwards, they were amended to the extent required to take
account of the re-constituting of the patents count court as the Intellectual Property Enterprise Court (see para.45.30.1). The
supplementing directions in Section 25C of the Costs Practice Directions were re-issued in Practice Direction 45 (paras 3.1 to
3.3). For texts of these provisions, see para.45PD.3.
The rules in Section VIII of the pre-April 2013 Pt 45 (rr.45.44 to 45.47) were reenacted in the post-April 2013 Pt 45 as Section
V (rr.45.33 to 45.36) without substantive amendment. For texts of these rules, see para.45.33. These rules were not, and are
not, subject to supplementing directions.
