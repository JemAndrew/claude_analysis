Civil Legal Aid (Statutory Charge) Regulations 2013
Part 2 Operation of the Statutory Charge
10.— Operation of the statutory charge where certain determinations are withdrawn
7D-87+
(1) Paragraph (2) applies where a determination in relation to which a certificate was issued is withdrawn.
(2) Where this paragraph applies, the statutory charge applies to property recovered or preserved by, or costs payable
to, the legally aided party (or their personal representatives, trustee in bankruptcy or the Official Receiver) after the
withdrawal of the determination in the proceedings or dispute in relation to which the determination was made.
