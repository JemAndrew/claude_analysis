Rule 43_3
43.3 Meaning of summary assessment
7B-3+
“Summary assessment” means the procedure by which the court, when making an order about costs, orders payment of
a sum of money instead of fixed costs or “detailed assessment”.
Summary assessment where there is “additional liability”
7B-3. 1+ See Section 3 of the Costs Practice Direction (para.7BPD-2+).
