Action for breach of condition or warranty
Trivial defects
25-33
A new provision, SGA s.15A, was added in 1994 for non-consumer sales. Where a buyer would otherwise have the right to
reject goods for breaches of ss.13, 14 or 15, the breach will be treated as a breach of warranty and not as a breach of condition
if “the breach is so slight that it would be unreasonable for him to reject them”.
