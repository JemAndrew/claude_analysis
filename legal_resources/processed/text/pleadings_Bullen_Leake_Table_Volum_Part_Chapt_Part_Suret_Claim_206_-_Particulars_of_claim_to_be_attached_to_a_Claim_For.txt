Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
20-F21
By an agreement in writing dated [date], (“the loan agreement”), the Claimant agreed to advance to the First Defendant
the sum of £[..........]. A copy of the loan agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
The said loan with interest of £[..........] was payable by x equal consecutive monthly instalments of £[..........].
3.
By a guarantee in writing dated [date], (“the guarantee”), the Second Defendant agreed that, in consideration of the
Claimant granting the loan to the First Defendant, the Second Defendant guaranteed the due performance by the First
Defendant of his obligations under the loan agreement. A copy of the guarantee is annexed to this Claim Form.
4.
The loan agreement and the guarantee were regulated by the Consumer Credit Act 1974 and this is a Consumer Credit
Act claim.
5.
The First Defendant failed to pay the instalments due from [date] to [date] inclusive in the total sum of £[..........] or any
part thereof.
6.
On [date] the Claimant served on the First Defendant a default notice requiring payment of the said sum of £[..........]
within 14 days and on the same date served a copy of the said notice on the Second Defendant. Copies of those notices
are annexed to these Particulars of Claim.
