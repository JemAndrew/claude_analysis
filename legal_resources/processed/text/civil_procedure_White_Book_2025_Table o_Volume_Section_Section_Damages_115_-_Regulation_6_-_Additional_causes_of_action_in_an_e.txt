Damages-Based Agreements Regulations 2013
Regulation 6
6. Additional causes of action in an employment matter
7A-32
In an employment matter, any amendment to a damages-based agreement to cover additional causes of action must be in
writing and signed by the client and the representative.
Editorial note
7A-32. 1 Regulation 6 adds a further requirement, specific to employment cases, that amendments to agreements so as to add additional
causes of action must be in writing and signed by the client and the representative.
