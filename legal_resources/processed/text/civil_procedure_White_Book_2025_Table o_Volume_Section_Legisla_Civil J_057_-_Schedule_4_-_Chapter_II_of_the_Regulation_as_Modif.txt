Section 5 - International Jurisdiction and Enforcement of Judgments
Part - Legislation
Civil Jurisdiction and Judgments Act 1982
Schedule 4 Chapter II of the Regulation as Modified: Rules for Allocation of Jurisdiction within UK
General
5-52
1. Subject to the rules of this Schedule, persons domiciled in a part of the United Kingdom shall be sued in the courts
of that part.
2. Persons domiciled in a part of the United Kingdom may be sued in the courts of another part of the United Kingdom
only by virtue of rules 3 to 13 of this Schedule.
