Particulars of claim to be attached to a Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
20-F12
By a hire agreement in writing dated [date], the Claimant let to the Defendant on hire [identify the goods] (“the goods”).
A copy of the agreement is annexed to these Particulars of Claim.
2.
The term of the hire was for x months and the rent was £[..........] per month payable monthly on the first day of the month.
3.
Clause 3 of the agreement provided that the Defendant should pay interest of x% over PQ Bank Plc base rate on all overdue
sums.
4.
Clause 10 of the agreement provided that, if the Defendant should fail to pay the rent when it fell due, the Claimant should
be entitled by notice to determine the agreement and the hiring.
5.
Clause 11 provided that, in the event of the Claimant terminating the agreement under clause 10, the Defendant should pay
to the Claimant, in addition to any sums currently due, a sum equivalent to the balance of rent due under the agreement
discounted down to the date of termination at the rate of x%.
6.
In breach of the agreement the Defendant failed to pay the rent due on [date] or any rent thereafter. On [date] the rent
was £[..........] in arrear.

At the date of the issue of these proceedings the value of the goods was £[..........].

(1)Delivery up of [identify the goods] or payment of £[..........] its value.
 
(2)£[..........] arrears of rent.

(4)£[..........] contractual interest accrued from [date when each sum became due] to the date of the issue of the claim
and continuing at the daily rate of £[..........] until judgment
