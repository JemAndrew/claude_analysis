Rule 45_12
45.12— Claims for an amount of costs exceeding fixed recoverable costs 1
7B-27+
(1) The court will entertain a claim for an amount of costs (excluding any success fee or disbursements) greater than the
fixed recoverable costs but only if it considers that there are exceptional circumstances making it appropriate to do so.
(2) If the court considers such a claim appropriate, it may—
(a) assess the costs; or
(b) make an order for the costs to be assessed.
(3) If the court does not consider the claim appropriate, it must make an order for fixed recoverable costs only.
Effect of rule
7B-27.
1+
This rule applies in relation to a “pre-commencement funding arrangement”; see para.7B-19+, and paras 48.0.1 to 48.0.4
(“Continuing application of CPR Pts 43 to 48 and Costs Practice Direction as in force immediately before 1 April 2013”).
