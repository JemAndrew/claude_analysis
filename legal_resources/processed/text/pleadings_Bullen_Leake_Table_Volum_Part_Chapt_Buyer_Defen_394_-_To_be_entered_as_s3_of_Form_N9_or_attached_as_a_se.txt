The Defendant admits that he entered into the oral agreement with the Claimant whereby the Claimant agreed to sell to
the Defendant the goods referred to in the Particulars of Claim.

(a)of satisfactory quality;
 
(b)reasonably fit for the purpose for which the Defendant required it, namely [set out purpose relied on].

In breach of the said conditions, the goods were not of satisfactory quality nor were they reasonably fit for the said purpose.
