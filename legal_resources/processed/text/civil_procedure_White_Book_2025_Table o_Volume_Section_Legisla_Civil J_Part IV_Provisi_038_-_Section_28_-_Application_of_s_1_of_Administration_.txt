Section 28
Part IV Miscellaneous Provisions
Provisions relating to jurisdiction
28. Application of s. 1 of Administration of Justice (Scotland) Act 1972.
5-32
(1)  When any proceedings have been brought, or are likely to be brought, in a 2005 Hague Convention State or
in England and Wales or Northern Ireland in respect of any matter which is within the scope of the Regulation as
determined in Article 1 , the Court of Session shall have the like power to make an order under section 1 of the
Administration of Justice (Scotland) Act 1972 as amended by the Law Reform (Miscellaneous Provisions)(Scotland)
Act 1985 as if the proceedings in question had been brought, or were likely to be brought, in that court.
