Claim for commission and an account (outside the 1993 Regulations)
Particulars of claim to be attached in Claim Form N1 or served subsequently
1.
21-F1
By a written agreement made on the [date] the Defendant appointed the Claimant its sole agent for the sale of [identify
goods] in [identify geographical area]. A copy of the agency agreement is annexed to this Claim Form.
2.
It was an express term of the agency agreement that the Claimant was entitled to be paid commission at the rate of x% of
the sale price on sales made by the Defendant which were effected through the agency of the Claimant.
3.
It was also an express term of the agency agreement that the Defendant was to provide the Claimant with details of all
such sales in a written statement given in the month following the sales and that the commission due thereon was payable
on the last day of that month.
4.
On [date], the Defendant sold [identify goods] to X Limited of [address] at a price of £[..........] on which commission was
due to the Claimant in the sum of £[..........]. The sale was effected through the agency of the Claimant.
5.
The written statement for [date] omitted to refer to the sale to X Limited or that commission was payable to the Claimant
thereon. Thereafter, the Defendant has failed to provide the Claimant with written statements of sales made in the preceding
month on which commission is payable to the Claimant.
6.
The Claimant is entitled to an account of all sales effected by the Defendant through the Claimant’s agency since the date
of the agreement and an order for the payment of commission found due upon the taking of the account.
7.
As a result of the sale to X Limited, the Claimant is entitled to be paid commission by the Defendant of £[..........].
